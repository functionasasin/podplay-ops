# Cheerful SSR Consumer Simulation

Idea from brainstorming session. Apply the Semantic Similarity Rating (SSR) methodology from [arxiv:2510.08338](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.08338) to Cheerful's marketing flows.

## Core Concept
Use LLM-simulated consumer panels to pre-test and optimize marketing decisions before executing them. SSR elicits textual responses from LLMs and maps them to Likert distributions via embedding similarity — achieving ~90% of human test-retest reliability.

## Potential Applications
- Campaign pre-testing: simulate consumer reactions to briefs/messaging/creatives before sending to influencers
- Influencer-audience matching: simulate how an audience would react to a brand/product
- Full funnel optimization: simulate response at every stage (awareness → consideration → intent → purchase)
