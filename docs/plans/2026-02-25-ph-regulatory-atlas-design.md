# Philippine Regulatory Atlas — Design Doc

**Date:** 2026-02-25
**Status:** Approved
**Loop:** `loops/ph-regulatory-atlas-reverse/`

## Context

The `ph-compliance-moats-reverse` loop surveyed 13 Philippine legal codes and identified 41 automation-ripe compliance domains. However, it only covered a subset of PH regulatory verticals: NIRC, Labor Code, Corporation Code, Family Code, Civil Code, Insurance Code, BIR forms, SEC filings, DOLE compliance, LGU real property, and Maceda Law.

This loop covers everything else — an exhaustive atlas of every Philippine regulatory body and statutory code not already surveyed.

## Thesis

Same as the original: find domains where (1) the math is statutory/deterministic, (2) professionals gatekeep access, (3) large populations are affected, and (4) the current process is painful.

## Approach: Exhaustive Regulatory Atlas (4 Waves)

### Wave 0 — Build the Registry (1 aspect)

Enumerate every Philippine executive department, attached agency, GOCC, constitutional commission, independent body, and major statutory code. Assess each for citizen-facing compliance requirements and computability potential. Generate Wave 1 aspects from the registry.

### Wave 1 — Source Scanning (N aspects, generated by Wave 0)

Expected 30-50+ aspects covering verticals including but not limited to:
- **Social protection:** SSS, GSIS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG/HDMF, OWWA
- **Trade & customs:** BOC (CMTA), DTI, PEZA, BOI
- **Land & agrarian:** DAR (RA 6657/CARP), DENR, LRA, NHA, DHSUD
- **Banking & finance:** BSP, AMLC, SEC (non-corporate), IC (non-code)
- **Energy:** ERC, NEA, DOE
- **Cooperatives:** CDA (RA 9520)
- **Education:** CHED, DepEd, TESDA
- **Professional regulation:** PRC
- **Transport:** LTO, LTFRB, MARINA, CAAP/CAB
- **Overseas workers:** DMW/POEA, OWWA
- **Telecoms & digital:** NTC, DICT, NPC (Data Privacy)
- **Health:** DOH, FDA, PhilHealth (provider-side)
- **Competition & consumer:** PCC, DTI (consumer)
- **Environment:** DENR/EMB, LLDA
- **Indigenous peoples:** NCIP
- **IP & technology:** IPOPHL
- **Agriculture & fisheries:** DA, BFAR
- **Gaming:** PAGCOR, PCSO
- **Elections:** COMELEC (campaign finance)
- **Audit & accountability:** COA, Ombudsman

### Wave 2 — Cross-Reference & Scoring (3 aspects)

Deduplicate (including cross-reference with the original 41 domains), score on market/moat/computability/pain, validate professional fees via WebSearch.

### Wave 3 — Synthesis (1 aspect)

Ranked shortlist with top 5 candidates for dedicated loops, plus a unified atlas merging both surveys.

## Exclusions

All domains already covered by:
- `ph-compliance-moats-reverse` (41 domains across 13 sources)
- `estate-tax-reverse` (PH estate tax)
- `inheritance-reverse` (PH succession law)

## Configuration

- Max iterations: 100 (no artificial limit — run until convergence)
- Timeout per iteration: 1800s (30 min)
- Failure tolerance: 3 consecutive failures

## Expected Output

- `analysis/wave0-registry.md` — Complete PH regulatory body inventory
- `analysis/wave1-*.md` — One file per agency/code scanned
- `analysis/master-domain-list.md` — Deduplicated domain inventory
- `analysis/scored-domains.md` — Scored and ranked domains
- `analysis/ranked-shortlist.md` — Final output with top candidates and unified atlas
