# Thorn — NnB Restructuring Reaction

Casual text conversation with [[Thorn]] after [[Flint]] and [[Slate]] were let go from [[Nuts and Bolts]].

## Context

Thorn found out that morning after 1-on-1s. Was surprised ("got gulat").

## Key Points from Conversation

- **Cash flow**: Some deals didn't push through, bleeding cash. Didn't make sense to have that many engineers on Cheerful.
- **Not consulted**: I wasn't consulted on the decision to fire them.
- **Quartz's hubris**: Quartz got arrogant ("yumabang") with the PRs he's pushing. Doesn't understand that Slate and Flint set up the codebase in a way that enables him to push PRs like that.
- **Thorn shares the vibe**: Thorn has always had that feeling about Quartz. Quartz is also the reason Thorn almost got fired before.
- **Quartz's motivation**: Just got married, pinning all his hopes and dreams on Cheerful. Probably didn't get the same "obsessed with Cheerful" vibe from Flint and Slate.
- **Horse blinders**: Aspen and Quartz are thinking too narrowly. Should have kept at least one of Flint/Slate as insurance to keep the codebase sanitary for LLMs to work effectively.
- **Survival mode vs leadership**: I get it's survival mode and they need to please customers, but I've lost faith in them as leaders on the Cheerful team.
- **My resolve**: If Cheerful takes off, it's all in my hands. I have plans to see it through. "Gloves off time" — so the sacrifice of the two isn't in vain.

## About Flint and Slate

- Both are talented ("galing"). Thorn thinks it's a waste ("sayang") to let them go.
- Slate has a lot of successful side projects — he'll be fine.
- Flint and I have another side thing going.
- Thorn thought it was weird they weren't moved elsewhere in the org instead of being let go entirely.
