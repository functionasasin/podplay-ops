Casual text conversation with [[Paolo]] after [[Derick]] and [[Josh]] were let go from [[Nuts and Bolts]].

## Context

Paolo found out that morning after 1-on-1s. Was surprised ("got gulat").

- **Paul's hubris**: Paul got arrogant ("yumabang") with the PRs he's pushing. Doesn't understand that Josh and Derick set up the codebase in a way that enables him to push PRs like that.

- Josh has a lot of successful side projects — he'll be fine.
- Derick and I have another side thing going.
- Paolo thought it was weird they weren't moved elsewhere in the org instead of being let go entirely.
