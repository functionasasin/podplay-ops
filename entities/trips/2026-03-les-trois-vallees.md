### Snowboard Tips for 3 Vallées

- **Wax aggressively** for the temperature — single most important tip for the flat traverses
- **Avoid:** bottom of Combe du Vallon (take Campagnol), Boulevard Cumin, Villages/Les Allues run, Courchevel Moriond/Canyon de Yepa, La Truite green
- **Build speed** before any connecting traverse — flat sections are the bane of snowboarders here
- **Weight front foot** when flat-basing
- **Last Olympe gondola:** ~5:00-5:30pm. Be at La Chaudanne by 4:45pm. Missing it means an expensive taxi
- **Liberty Ride zones** are your friend — secured off-piste, no avalanche risk, no guide needed. Five locations across the area

### Parking (If Renting a Car)

Free parking is available in Brides-les-Bains. You will not use the car during riding days — the Olympe gondola handles everything. Car is mainly useful for:
- Arrival/departure airport transfers
- Grocery runs to Moûtiers Intermarché (10 min drive, much bigger selection + cheaper)
- Optional rest-day excursions
