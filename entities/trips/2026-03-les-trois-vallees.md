**Car pros:** Freedom to explore other resorts/valleys, supermarket runs to Moûtiers, day trips (Paradiski, Tignes), no schedule dependency, free parking in Brides-les-Bains.

### Option B: No Car (shared bus transfer)

| Item | Cost (€) | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Flights MNL↔GVA (round trip) | €700 | Same as above |
| Shared bus transfer GVA↔Brides (return) | €110 | Ben's Bus, Sat/Sun only |
| Accommodation — studio, 2 weeks | €700 | Same |
| 3 Vallées Pass — 2 × 6-day | €818 | Same |
| Snowboard hire — 2 weeks | €180 | Same |
| Food — self-catered, 14 days | €450 | Same |
| Tourist tax — 14 nights | €42 | Same |
| Extras & incidentals | €200 | Same |
| **TOTAL** | **~€3,200** | |

**No-car pros:** ~€540 cheaper, no driving stress on mountain roads, no fuel/toll hassle. Free Olympe gondola to Méribel + free shuttle buses within each valley cover daily skiing needs.

**No-car cons:** Locked to Sat/Sun arrivals for shared bus, grocery runs limited to Brides village, can't easily day-trip outside 3 Vallées, harder to explore Moûtiers supermarkets for cheaper bulk food.

### Comparison Summary

| | With Car | No Car | Difference |
|---|----------|--------|------------|
| Transport costs | €1,352 | €810 | +€542 |
| On-mountain costs | €2,390 | €2,390 | €0 |
| **Total** | **€3,742** | **€3,200** | **+€542** |

The car adds ~17% to total cost. Worth it mainly if you want flexibility to explore beyond the 3 Vallées or prefer driving over bus schedules. For a pure 2-week snowboard immersion staying in Brides, the no-car option is solid — free shuttles and the Olympe gondola handle everything.

## Money-Saving Tips

- Book Brides-les-Bains or Orelle for 30-50% savings
- Family Flex Pass: everyone pays child rate (€335/6 days)
- Book equipment online for up to 40% off
- Travel late March (after 20th) for 10-15% discounts
- Self-catering saves €200-300 vs half-board
