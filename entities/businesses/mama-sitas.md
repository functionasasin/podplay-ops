## Strategic Role: Brand-Side Dogfooding

This campaign doubles as proof-of-concept for [[cheerful-brand-first-strategy]]. Running Mama Sita's through Cheerful *as a brand* (not an agency) surfaces friction points that agency-only feedback never would. See the brand-first strategy idea for the full thesis and friction tracking checklist.

Key brand-perspective insights to surface:
- Outreach quality (does it represent the brand voice?)
- Creator-product fit (not just demographics — brand alignment)
- Post-reply lifecycle (shipping, tracking, posting confirmation)
- UGC capture and reuse
- Repeat engagement and creator relationship intelligence
