# Magpie

Payment platform partner in the Philippines for [[Pod Play]].

## Status

- Payment integration is working: GCash and credit card payments functional
- Successfully processed a test booking (fictitious court, real money)
- Currently developing at no cost to the team — they see strategic value in the wallet opportunity

## Wallet Opportunity

- Magpie is excited about the digital wallet potential
- If Pod Play scales, the wallet becomes a significant asset
- Magpie wants to be part of the wallet ecosystem
- Regulatory license is Philippines-only, but they have counterpart partners in other Asian markets
- Technology can be replicated with local partners elsewhere

## Negotiation Position

- Plan to push for 80/20 split on wallet, expecting to settle around 50/50
- They have regulatory filings and compliance — team does not
- Partnership is mutually beneficial: Magpie has tech + regulation, team has market + distribution
