# Ping Pod

Table tennis / ping pong venue business. Originally one company with [[Pod Play]], then split into two separate entities.

## Investment History

- Team invested pre-Series A after [[Sequoia]] offered the opportunity
- Ping Pod raised an $8M Series A from a fund with strategic value (franchising/technology focus)
- A second round of fundraising was initiated for Ping Pod after the split; team invested again

## Franchise Model

- **Country franchise fee**: $100K
- **Per-store fee**: $28K
- **Expansion requirements**: 10 stores in year 1, 30 stores in year 2
- Ping Pod cannot operate without [[Pod Play]] software — creates strong lock-in
- [[Central Group]] (Thailand) interested in being master franchisor

## Southeast Asia Strategy

- Team holds [[Pod Play]] distribution rights for Southeast Asia
- Franchise opportunities being explored across the region
- Singapore being considered as Asia HQ (see [[Pod Play SEA]])
