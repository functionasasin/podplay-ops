# Ping Pod Asia Franchise

Master franchise distribution for [[Ping Pod]] venues across Asia.

## Franchise Model

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Country franchise fee | $100K |
| Per-store fee | $28K |
| Year 1 requirement | 10 stores |
| Year 2 requirement | 30 stores |

## Strategic Lock-in

Ping Pod venues **require** [[Pod Play]] software to operate. Since the team controls Pod Play in Southeast Asia, every Ping Pod franchise deal doubles as a Pod Play deployment.

## Thailand Opportunity

- [[Central Group]] (one of Thailand's largest conglomerates) interested in being master franchisor
- A Vietnam-based group introduced Central Group to the opportunity
- Approval is up to the team — [[Zoe]] confirmed with [[Heath]]
- If approved, franchise fee alone is significant revenue
