## Overview

The **Q City Bus (QCityBus)** is the Quezon City local government's free public bus service. It started in 2020 as a COVID transport augmentation and became permanent via **Ordinance No. SP-3184, S-2023** (June 2023), signed by Mayor Joy Belmonte. Service is free (₱0) to all passengers.

- **Operator**: Quezon City Government / Office of the City Administrator (OCA) / Transport and Traffic Management Department (TTMD)
- **Fleet**: ~100 buses across 8 routes
- **Electric buses**: 8 units procured October 2024; 6 deployed Jan 2, 2025 on Route 1
- **Fare**: ₱0 (free to all — QCitizens, workers, students transacting in QC)
- **Total ridership**: 41+ million as of October 2025 since 2020
- **Operating days**: Daily except public holidays
- **Passengers per bus**: 41 seated; no standing passengers allowed
- **Features**: Air-conditioned, free WiFi, CCTV, wheelchair ramps (e-bus), folding bikes allowed
