## Overview

Monumento Circle is Metro Manila's primary northern transit hub, located in South Caloocan at the confluence of four major roads: **EDSA**, **MacArthur Highway**, **Samson Road**, and **Rizal Avenue Extension** (the historic road to Manila city proper).

**Geographic center**: ~14.6553°N, 120.9841°E (Rizal Ave Ext / Samson Rd / EDSA intersection, Caloocan)

The area has two distinct transportation functions:
1. **Northern terminus** for many Manila-bound routes (buses and jeepneys heading south on Rizal Ave and EDSA)
2. **Southern terminus** for routes originating in Caloocan's hinterland and Bulacan province (MacArthur Highway routes heading north)

The Monumento LRT-1 station is physically connected to SM City Grand Central (formerly Ever Gotesco Grand Central), making it also an interchange point with LRT-1.

## Terminal Inventory

### 1. EDSA Carousel Terminal (Monumento)
- **Location**: EDSA at Monumento Circle, Caloocan
- **Function**: Northern terminal of the EDSA Carousel BRT system
- **Routes**: EDSA Carousel Route E (Monumento ↔ PITX, Parañaque), 24/7 service
- **Connections**: Adjacent to SM City Grand Central, walkable to LRT-1 Monumento Station
- **Notes**: Northernmost point of the BRT system; 24 southbound stops to PITX (~28km)

### 2. Araneta Square Mall Terminal (Samson Road)
- **Location**: Araneta Square Mall, 524 Rizal Ave corner Samson Road, Caloocan
- **Function**: City bus terminal (southbound routes, Rizal Ave direction)
- **Routes**: Route 17 (Monumento–EDSA Taft), Route 3 (Monumento–VGC)
- **Also home to**: Victory Liner provincial terminal (Olongapo/Zambales routes)

### 3. Bonifacio Market Terminal (MacArthur Highway)
- **Location**: Bonifacio Market, MacArthur Highway, Caloocan City
- **Function**: City bus staging area (northbound routes to Bulacan)
- **Routes**: Route 20 (Monumento–Meycauayan), Route 22 (Monumento–Angat)
- **Notes**: Distinct from Araneta Square Mall terminal; serves MacArthur Highway corridor

### 4. 5th Avenue / EDSA Corner Terminal
- **Location**: Epifanio de los Santos Avenue / 5th Street Intersection, Caloocan City
- **Function**: City bus terminal (alternative Monumento staging)
- **Routes**: Route 22 extended variant (EDSA entry via Balintawak/Cloverleaf to NLEX)
- **Notes**: Buses board here for provincial-bound NLEX routes

### 5. Victory Liner Terminal
- **Address**: 524 Rizal Avenue corner Samson Road (or 713 Rizal Avenue Extension), Caloocan
- **Contact**: (02) 361-1506, (02) 361-4665 to 66
- **Function**: Provincial bus terminal (Luzon north routes)
- **Accessibility**: 52m from Ever Gotesco Grand Central; 118m from Monumento LRT stop

### 6. Street-Side Jeepney Terminals
Multiple informal staging zones around the rotunda:
- **Rizal Ave Extension** (southbound toward Manila): jeepneys to Divisoria, Quiapo, Recto, Baclaran
- **MacArthur Highway** (northbound): jeepneys to Malabon, Navotas, Valenzuela, Bocaue, Marilao, Meycauayan
- **Samson Road / EDSA** (west): jeepneys to Malabon via Letre/Acacia, Navotas
- **St. Michael and Malinta Exit Jeepney Terminal**: Distinct staging area for Malinta/Valenzuela routes

## Fare Data

### City Bus (Routes 1, 3, 9, 17, 20, 21, 22, 37)
- Ordinary: ₱13 base + ₱2.25/km
- Air-conditioned: ₱15 base + ₱2.65/km
- Route 1 example (Monumento→Balagtas, ~45km): ~₱115 AC
- Route 22 example (Monumento→Angat, ~55km): ~₱55 (estimated from ₱55 fare cited)

### EDSA Carousel
- Min: ₱15 (short trips); Max: ₱74.50 (Monumento↔PITX, full E2E)
- Payment: Beep card, GCash (GCash accepted from Dec 2025)

### Jeepney
- Traditional: ₱13 base + ₱1.80/km
- Modern PUJ: ₱15 base + ₱2.20/km
- Bocaue estimate: ~₱30–40 (MacArthur Hwy jeepney, ~15km)
- Meycauayan estimate: ~₱25–35

- Olongapo: ~₱285
- Dagupan: ~₱392
- Baguio: ~₱645+
