## Overview

The Pasay Rotonda (also called EDSA-Taft or MRT-3 Taft) is the southernmost major transit interchange on EDSA. It anchors at the intersection of EDSA and Taft Avenue in Pasay City and is one of Metro Manila's densest multi-modal hubs, serving:

- **Rail**: MRT-3 Taft Avenue Station (terminal/southern end) + LRT-1 EDSA Station
- **BRT**: EDSA Carousel (Heritage Hotel stop + MRT Taft stop)
- **Provincial buses**: 15+ operators departing for Northern/Southern Luzon and Visayas
- **City buses**: Local routes to Cavite, plus EDSA Carousel
- **Jeepneys**: 15+ local routes to southern Manila, Makati, NAIA, and Cavite
- **UV Express/Vans**: Multiple terminals going to Cavite province

**Geographic center**: EDSA cor Taft Avenue, Pasay City (~14.5504°N, 121.0019°E)

## Terminal Inventory

### 1. EDSA Carousel Stops
- **Heritage Hotel Stop**: On EDSA median, curbside platform; serves PITX-Monumento route (24-hour service from Taft stop, extended)
- **MRT Taft Stop**: On EDSA median; southbound connects to PITX; northbound serves Ayala and beyond
- **Fare**: ₱15 from PITX to Heritage/MRT Taft (minimum fare zone); integrated with BEEP card

### 2. MRT-3 Taft Avenue Station
- Terminal station of MRT-3 (southernmost)
- Connects to EDSA Carousel, jeepneys, UV Express, and LRT-1 via pedestrian concourse
- MRT-3 fares: ₱13–₱28 (distance-based); BEEP card accepted

### 3. LRT-1 EDSA Station
- LRT-1 station connecting to Baclaran (1 stop south) and Roosevelt/FPJ (northbound terminus)
- Adjacent to MRT-3 Taft; walk connection via overpass
- LRT-1 fares: ₱15–₱43 (Stored Value), ₱15–₱45 (SJT) — new Nov 2024 fare matrix

### 4. Genesis Bus Terminal (below MRT Taft)
- **Location**: Below MRT-3 Taft Avenue Station, entrance beside McDonald's LRT EDSA
- **Operators**: Genesis Bus (Bataan/Mariveles); San Agustin Bus (Nasugbu, Batangas)
- **San Agustin schedule**: 3:00 AM – 9:00 PM

### P2P Premium Bus

No dedicated P2P terminal at Pasay Rotonda. PITX (Parañaque Integrated Terminal Exchange), ~4 km southwest of Pasay Rotonda, is the primary P2P hub. The EDSA Carousel connects Pasay Rotonda to PITX (Heritage/MRT Taft → PITX direction). Some P2P routes (UBE Express) pick up/drop off at Heritage Hotel along EDSA.
