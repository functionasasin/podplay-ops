## Terminal Overview

SM North EDSA / Trinoma is one of Metro Manila's largest transit hubs, located at the EDSA–North Avenue intersection in Quezon City. The complex encompasses several distinct sub-terminals:

1. **SM City North EDSA main terminal** — city buses, P2P, provincial vans
2. **Trinoma terminal** — P2P buses (Bulacan), intra-Metro P2P
3. **SM North EDSA FX Terminal** — UV Express / FX vans
4. **EDSA Carousel concourse** — new busway concourse opened March 2025, directly links SM North EDSA to the EDSA Carousel median stations
5. **SM North EDSA FX Terninal** (Moovit site ID: 29497899) — separately listed stop near Quirino Highway

Nearby rail: **MRT-3 North Avenue Station** (within the SM–Trinoma complex), and **LRT-1 Roosevelt Station** (~20 min walk north along EDSA).

Operating hours: ~3:00 AM – midnight for most services; EDSA Carousel concourse 24/7.

## EDSA Carousel

SM North EDSA / North Avenue is a confirmed EDSA Carousel stop.

- **Stop type**: Median station with elevator/escalator access (confirmed accessible station)
- **New concourse**: March 2025 — SM City North EDSA opened a dedicated concourse connecting directly to the Carousel
- **Fare from PITX**: ₱60 (one-way, cash/Beep Card)
- **Fare from Monumento**: ~₱15 (minimum, short hop)
- **End-to-end Monumento–PITX**: ₱74.50–₱75.50
- **OSM relation**: 10183711 (EDSA Carousel)

## Key Findings

1. **SM North EDSA / Trinoma is a dual terminal**: SM North EDSA serves P2P, city buses, UV Express; Trinoma serves Bulacan P2P and intra-Metro P2P. Routes often serve both (e.g., Precious Grace has both SM North and Trinoma variants).

2. **Bulacan P2P dominates the terminal**: Precious Grace Transport operates the densest route network (8 distinct origin–destination pairs across Bocaue, Caypombo, Malolos, Sta. Clara, Pandi, Balagtas, Plaridel), all terminating at SM North EDSA or Trinoma.

3. **Jeepney routes are partially disrupted**: The OSM-tagged SM North EDSA ↔ Katipunan route is marked disused (likely post-pandemic modernization). Active jeepney routes to Sauyo, Balintawak, and Mindanao Ave areas confirmed via Moovit frequency data (every 5 min headways).

4. **New EDSA Carousel concourse** (March 2025) improves connectivity — SM North EDSA is now directly accessible from the EDSA Busway without crossing surface roads.

5. **MRT-3 North Avenue is the anchor**: The entire terminal complex is oriented around MRT-3 North Avenue Station as the rail transfer point.

6. **Froelich Tours fares need 2025 verification**: Current data is 2018–2019 vintage. The operator may have adjusted fares under LTFRB MC 2023-038.
