## Terminal Overview

The Fairview area in northern Quezon City is a major transit hub anchored at **SM City Fairview** (corner Quirino Highway and Regalado Highway, QC). It is the northernmost large transit hub in Quezon City proper, serving as the de facto terminus for routes from Metro Manila's inner districts heading north.

**Distinct terminal zones at SM Fairview:**
1. **SM Fairview Public Transport Terminal A** — Regalado Highway, QC (main multi-modal hub; BUS, JEEP, P2P)
2. **SM Fairview Public Transport Terminal B** — Belfast Avenue, QC (secondary bus/jeepney terminal)
3. **SM Fairview Jeepney Terminal** — Regalado Highway, QC (JEEP, UV Express)
4. **Robinsons Novaliches / Fairview Center Mall** — Quirino Highway, Caloocan (300–402m walk from SM Fairview; functions as satellite terminal, used by RRCG P2P)

The SM Public Transport Terminal is ~1 minute walk from the SM Fairview Jeepney Terminal. Robinsons Nova Supermarket/Novaliches (~301–385m walk) also has bus stop access.

**No rail connection**: Nearest MRT-3 station is North Ave (~8km via Commonwealth Ave). No direct rail-to-Fairview connection currently.

## Geographic Notes

**Key road corridors from Fairview:**
- **Commonwealth Avenue** (southbound): Main trunk road toward Quezon Memorial Circle, Elliptical, Philcoa, España, then Manila. Carries most jeepney, bus, UV Express heading south to Manila/Makati.
- **Quirino Highway** (runs east-west along SM Fairview / Regalado intersection): Links Caloocan-Fairview-Novaliches-North Fairview. UV Express and local jeepney use this.
- **Regalado Highway**: Connects SM Fairview terminal area south-southwest to North Fairview, Batasan Hills, Lagro.
- **Belfast Avenue**: Secondary road linking Terminal B; connects via Maligaya to Bagong Silang (Caloocan).

**Key destinations served:**
- **Southbound** (city-bound): Quiapo, Divisoria, Lawton, Recto, España/Manila, Monumento, QMC, Philcoa, Cubao
- **Central hubs**: SM North EDSA, Trinoma, Cubao, Quezon Avenue, Eton Centris
- **CBDs**: Makati (RRCG P2P, UV, city bus via EDSA), Ortigas (COMET bus, UV transfer at Cubao), BGC (city bus)
- **Southernmost**: PITX, Baclaran (city bus via Commonwealth–EDSA–Taft)
- **North**: Norzagaray (cross-boundary jeepney), Novaliches, Lagro, Bagong Silang
