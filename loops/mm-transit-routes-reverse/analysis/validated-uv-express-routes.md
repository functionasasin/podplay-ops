UV Express (UVE) services operate AC vans (10–14 pax) on terminal-to-terminal fixed routes under LTFRB franchise. Fare: ₱2.00/km distance-based. Cannot use EDSA or Commonwealth as route corridors (crossing is permitted). Identified routes organized into geographic groups below.

## Route Code System

| Prefix | Scope | Example |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **N-xxx** | NCR internal (both ends in Metro Manila) | N08, N25, N52, N55, N64, N69, N72 |
| **C-xxx** | Cross-region (one end outside NCR) | C65, C66, C67, C68, C69 |

Only 7 N-codes and 5 C-codes are publicly confirmed from LTFRB MCs. Full index likely 100+ N-codes. Route codes are **not displayed on vehicles** — vehicles show text origin–destination.

## Coverage Gaps Identified

1. **Full N-code index unknown**: Only N08, N25, N52, N55, N64, N69, N72 confirmed from 100+ total N-codes. Routes N01–N07, N09–N24, N26–N51, N53–N54, N56–N63, N65–N68, N70–N71, N73+ not documented.
2. **Intramuros/Binondo area**: No UV routes documented for historic Manila districts.
3. **Mandaluyong internal**: Beyond EDSA Central as a terminus, no UVE routes originating from Mandaluyong documented.
4. **San Juan**: No UV routes found for San Juan City.
5. **Post-2024 consolidation status**: 108 NCR UV routes had no consolidated entity as of April 2024 LTFRB deadline. These may have ceased operations or continue in legal limbo.
6. **Makati terminal reorganization**: One Ayala terminal opened ~2023 absorbed routes from Dela Rosa, Valero, and Ayala Center terminals. Exact route reassignments not fully documented.
7. **Laguna (south)**: Only 3 Laguna routes captured; likely 10–15 routes from Calamba, Los Baños, Sta. Rosa, etc.
8. **Batangas/Quezon province**: Only 1 low-confidence Batangas route found (Pasay–Calatagan). More routes likely.
