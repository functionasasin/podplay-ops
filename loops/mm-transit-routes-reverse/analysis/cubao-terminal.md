## Overview

Cubao is Metro Manila's most complex transit interchange — the junction of MRT-3 (Farmers Station) and LRT-2 (Araneta Center–Cubao Station), plus the region's largest concentration of provincial bus terminals. All major transit modes converge here: provincial buses, local city buses, EDSA Carousel, modern jeepneys, traditional jeepneys, UV Express, and P2P services.

**Geographic center**: Intersection of EDSA and Aurora Boulevard, Quezon City (14.6237°N, 121.0527°E approx.)

---

## Terminal Inventory

### 1. Araneta City Bus Port (ACBP)
- **Address**: Ground Floor, Manhattan Heights, General Romulo Avenue, Socorro, Quezon City
- **Hours**: 5:00 AM – 11:00 PM daily
- **Capacity**: 19 bus bays; ~6,000 passengers/day
- **Function**: Provincial bus terminal + P2P bus hub; also hosts modern jeepney services
- **Contact**: +63 (02) 588-4150; busportcustomercare@aranetagroup.com

### 2. EDSA Bus Terminals (Northbound & Southbound)
- Multiple company-owned terminals strung along EDSA between Aurora Blvd and New York Avenue
- Northbound: Five Star (674 EDSA), Genesis+Joy Bus (683 EDSA), Victory Liner (683 EDSA), Solid North (676 EDSA), Baliwag Transit (EDSA NB)
- Southbound: ALPS (721 EDSA SB), Superlines/Daet Express (EDSA SB)
- Aurora Blvd side: DLTB (821 Aurora), Partas (812 Aurora), Dominion (832 Aurora cor Driod), JAC+JAM (2 Mapagmahal cor EDSA)
- Dagupan Bus: New York corner EDSA

### 3. Araneta City Jeepney Terminal (Traditional)
- **Location**: General Romulo Avenue, north of Araneta City (near Times Square Ave)
- **Function**: Traditional jeepney departures to QC + Rizal province

### 4. Times Square Avenue Terminal (Modern Jeepney Hub)
- **Location**: Old Araneta City Bus Terminal site, Times Square Avenue
- **Operator**: 1-TEAM (Transport Equipment Aggregator and Management Inc.) and partner cooperatives
- **Function**: Modern PUJ (BEEP jeepneys) to QC, Pasig, Marikina, Rizal, Bulacan

### 5. UV Express Terminals
- **Outside Farmers Plaza**: General Roxas Avenue near Jollibee — routes to Novaliches, Antipolo, Deparo
- **Facing Farmers Market**: Below LRT-2 Cubao Station — routes to Montalban, Parang, Cogeo, Balibago
- **Hours**: Varies by route; some 24/7, most 5AM–11PM

### 6. Street-Side Jeepney Stops
- **Aurora Blvd** (below LRT-2): jeepneys to Marikina, Cogeo, Padilla, Sta. Mesa, Divisoria, Quiapo
- **P. Tuazon Blvd** (near Ali Mall): jeepneys to Project 4, Antipolo, Taytay, Cainta
- **G. Romulo Avenue**: jeepneys to Pasig, Rosario, Eastwood, SM Fairview, Lagro, Philcoa

## Data Quality Assessment

**Strengths**:
- Terminal locations and operators well-documented across multiple sources
- Provincial bus operators and destinations highly reliable (stable, years-old information)
- Modern jeepney routes partially confirmed via Sakay.ph (DOTR:R_SAKAY_MPUJ_909)
- UV Express routes consistent across Araneta City website + community sources
- P2P route (UBE Express) confirmed via official UBE Express channels

**Gaps**:
- Traditional jeepney LTFRB T-series route codes NOT found for any Cubao route
- Modern jeepney routes: 14 identified but exact LTFRB MPUJ codes unknown (except MPUJ_909)
- Local bus route numbers: only Routes 51 and 53 confirmed terminating at Cubao; other routes passing through Cubao unconfirmed
- No GPS coordinates for any jeepney/UV Express stops at Cubao terminals
- Traditional jeepney routes from street terminals: destinations documented but no stop lists
- UV Express route codes (N-series or C-series) not found for Cubao-originating routes
- ACBP capacity/ridership data: 400–700 daily passengers (much lower than PITX) due to post-COVID redistribution

**Confidence**:
- Provincial bus routes: HIGH (stable, well-documented)
- EDSA Carousel Cubao stops: HIGH
- Modern jeepney destinations: MEDIUM (destination names confirmed, route codes unknown)
- Traditional jeepney destinations: MEDIUM (destination names confirmed from multiple sources)
- UV Express routes: MEDIUM (terminal locations + destinations confirmed, codes unknown)
- P2P (UBE Express): HIGH (official website + multiple confirmations)
- Local bus route numbers (51, 53): MEDIUM (mentioned but details sparse)
