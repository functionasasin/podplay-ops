# BGC Bus System

**Source type**: Private transit operator (official + secondary web sources)
**Retrieved**: 2026-02-28
**Coverage**: Bonifacio Global City (Taguig) + Makati + Arca South + Laguna (Nuvali)

---

## Overview

The **BGC Bus** (formerly The Fort Bus) is a private intercity bus system operated by **Bonifacio Transport Corporation**, a subsidiary of Fort Bonifacio Development Corporation (FBDC) under the **Ayala Corporation**. It is not a public-utility vehicle franchise under LTFRB — it operates as a private estate transport service within the BGC development.

The system serves Bonifacio Global City (BGC) as its hub, with routes extending to Makati CBD, LRT-1 Gil Puyat Station (Pasay), Arca South (Taguig), and — via premium express — Nuvali (Laguna).

### Key Characteristics
- **Flat fare**: ₱13–15 for most routes; ₱25 for Arca South Express; ₱90 for Nuvali Express
- **Cashless only**: Beep Card (tap-on tap-off) or GCash. One-time QR paper ticket available at terminals.
- **Operating hours**: Generally 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM weekdays; dedicated Night Route 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM
- **No LTFRB franchise**: Operates as private BRT within BGC estate; not captured in LTFRB route databases
- **BGC App**: iOS/Android app for real-time bus tracking and arrival estimates

## Routes (as of January 2025)

Sources vary on total count: most cite **8–10 active routes** as of January 1, 2025 schedule update. The discrepancy is due to some older sources listing now-consolidated or renamed routes.

### 1. East Express
- **Stops**: EDSA Ayala → Market! Market! (point-to-point, no intermediate stops)
- **Direction**: Bidirectional
- **Schedule**: Monday–Sunday, 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Fare**: ₱13–15
- **Notes**: The only route operating on weekends. Fastest connection between Makati and BGC. As of 2025, no longer stops at BGC Arts Center.

### 2. North Express
- **Stops**: EDSA Ayala → Arya Residences → NutriAsia → HSBC → BGC Turf → The Globe Tower → BGC Arts Center → (loop back to EDSA Ayala)
- **Stop count**: 8 stops (circular/loop route per Moovit)
- **Approximate trip time**: ~12 minutes
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday, 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Fare**: ₱13–15
- **Notes**: Serves the north portion of BGC (Uptown area via Arya Residences). Does NOT stop at The Fort Strip/RCBC. Different from the older "North Route" (10 stops starting from North Station).

### 3. Upper West Express
- **Stops**: EDSA Ayala → Bonifacio Stopover → Crescent Park West
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday, 6:00 AM – 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM (peak hours only per most sources; some sources indicate all day)
- **Fare**: ₱13–15
- **Notes**: Fastest way to access west BGC (Bonifacio Stopover, Crescent Park West) via Buendia Avenue / Kalayaan Flyover.

### 4. Lower West Express
- **Stops**: EDSA Ayala → McKinley Parkway → RCBC → Net One → Fort Victoria
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday, 6:00 AM – 10:00 AM and 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM (peak hours; some sources say all day)
- **Fare**: ₱13–15
- **Notes**: Serves west BGC via McKinley Road corridor. Stops at RCBC/Piccadilly Star and Fort Victoria residential area.

### 5. Central Route
- **Stops**: Market! Market! → NutriAsia → RCBC → Net One → Bonifacio Stopover → Crescent Park West → The Globe Tower → One Parkade → University Parkway (circular, returns to Market! Market!)
- **Stop count**: 8 named stops (per CashMart; BGC official page confirms same)
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday, 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Fare**: ₱13–15
- **Notes**: Circulates within BGC interior. The only route serving University Parkway from Market! Market!. Sources note it is slow during peak hours and walking may be faster within BGC core.

### 6. Night Route
- **Stops**: EDSA Ayala → McKinley Parkway → RCBC → Net One Center → Bonifacio Stopover → Crescent Park West → HSBC → The Globe Tower → NutriAsia → University Parkway → Market! Market! → One Parkade → [loop: back to] RCBC → Crescent Park West → Fort Victoria
- **Stop count**: ~15 stopovers (described as "almost circling the busiest part of BGC")
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday, 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM
- **Frequency**: Every 30 minutes
- **Fare**: ₱13–15
- **Notes**: Covers the widest geographic range within BGC among all routes. Serves late-night BGC workers and hospitality/BPO employees.

### 7. Weekend Route
- **Stops**: Same as Night Route (EDSA Ayala → [15 stops throughout BGC] → Fort Victoria)
- **Schedule**: Saturday–Sunday, 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Fare**: ₱13–15
- **Notes**: Weekends use the wider coverage Night Route pattern but during daytime hours.

### 8. Arca South Express
- **Stops**: Arca South → Market! Market! → NutriAsia → RCBC → Net One → Bonifacio Stopover → Crescent Park West → HSBC → The Globe Tower → One Parkade (also listed in reverse direction)
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday, 6:10 AM – 8:40 AM (AM peak) and 4:30 PM – 7:30 PM (PM peak)
- **Fare**: ₱25 (higher than standard ₱13–15)
- **Notes**: Connects Arca South development (south Taguig, near C6/Bayshore) to BGC. Peak hours only. Higher fare reflects longer distance and limited service windows.

### 9. Nuvali Express
- **Stops**: Nuvali (Laguna, SMDC area) → Market! Market!
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday only. Nuvali to BGC: 6:30 AM departure. BGC to Nuvali: 6:30 PM and 7:15 PM departures.
- **Fare**: ₱90 (₱72 PWD/senior discount)
- **Notes**: Premium long-distance express. Crosses provincial boundaries (NCR → Laguna). Very limited frequency — essentially one trip each direction per day per departure time. Not LTFRB-regulated as standard PUV.

### 10. Ayala Express
- **Stops**: EDSA Ayala → Ritz Tower → Makati Stock Exchange (MSE) → PBCom → RCBC Plaza → The Columns → City Gate → Security Bank → SGV Building → Glorietta 5
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday, 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Fare**: ₱13 (one source cites this older fare)
- **Notes**: Serves Makati CBD corridor (Ayala Avenue axis) — NOT BGC itself. Functions as a Makati CBD circulator connecting MRT-3 Ayala Station to the heart of Ayala CBD (Glorietta, SGV, RCBC Plaza area).

### 11. LRT-Ayala Route (LRT Buendia Route)
- **Stops**: LRT-1 Gil Puyat Station (Pasay) → Buendia Bus Terminal → Arellano University → ALPS Bus Terminal (in front of Sogo Hotel, Buendia) → One Ayala → Buendia–San Antonio Village Tricycle Terminal → [continues toward BGC]
- **Schedule**: Monday–Friday (specific hours not confirmed)
- **Fare**: ₱13–15 (assumed standard; not confirmed)
- **Notes**: Least documented route. Provides a direct connection from LRT-1 Gil Puyat Station (Buendia, Pasay) into BGC. Important intermodal link for commuters from south of Manila using LRT-1. Some sources list this as the "LRT-Ayala" route. The detailed stops within BGC are not confirmed from available sources.

## Source Conflicts & Discrepancies

| Issue | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Route count | Most sources say "8 routes"; CashMart lists 10; some January 2025 sources say 8 were restructured from previous 10+ |
| Fare ₱13 vs ₱15 | Older sources (2023) list ₱13; 2025 sources list ₱15. ₱15 appears to be the post-January 2025 rate for most routes. |
| "North Route" vs "North Express" | Some older sources describe a 10-stop "North Route" starting from "North Station" (Uptown area); current configuration appears to be the 8-stop "North Express" from EDSA Ayala. These may be the same route renamed or combined. |
| Upper/Lower West hours | Some sources say peak-only (6-10AM + 5-10PM); others say 6AM-10PM all day. Peak-only is more consistent across sources. |
| LRT-Ayala route detail | Stop list within BGC (beyond One Ayala) is not confirmed from available web sources. |
