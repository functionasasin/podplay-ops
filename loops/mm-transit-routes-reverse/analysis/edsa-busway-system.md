# EDSA Busway System — Analysis

**Aspect**: EDSA Busway system — all carousel routes, stops, schedules, fare structure
**Date**: 2026-02-25
**Sources**: edsacarousel.ph, edsacarousel.com, topgear.com.ph (Oct 2025), spot.ph (Oct 2025), sakay.ph, philippinebeaches.org (2026), DOTr, LTFRB

## Overview

The **EDSA Carousel** (officially **Route 1**, formerly Route E) is a Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) system operating on the **EDSA Busway** — a dedicated innermost lane separated from general traffic by concrete barriers and steel bollards. Interim operations began June 1, 2020 (pandemic augmentation); full operations July 1, 2020.

**Key metrics (as of 2025):**
- Route length: ~28 km end-to-end
- Daily ridership: ~180,000 passengers/day (Jan 2025 avg: 177,000)
- Total ridership: 63 million in 2024; 130 million cumulative since launch
- Authorized buses: 751 units across 87 operators
- Operating consortia: Mega Manila Consortium Corporation + ES Transport and Partners Consortium
- Regulator: DOTr / LTFRB

---

## Routes

There is currently **one primary route** (Route 1) on the EDSA Busway:

| Direction | Origin | Destination | Stops |
|-----------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Northbound | PITX (Parañaque) | Monumento (Caloocan) | 23 |
| Southbound | Monumento (Caloocan) | PITX (Parañaque) | 24 |

**Note**: Southbound has one more stop due to the Tramo intermediate stop and the One Ayala platform being separate from the northbound Ayala curbside stop.

No official sub-routes (Route 2, Route 3) were found. There is a **limited late-night service** from Heritage Hotel/MRT Taft (Pasay Rotonda) to Monumento operating 11 PM–4 AM (shorter sub-route).

**Planned expansion**: A new Cubao busway stop and two additional PITX stops expected by 2026 under the EDSA Busway Rehabilitation Project.

## Fare Structure

- **Type**: Distance-based, AC bus
- **Base fare**: ₱15.00 (first 5 km)
- **Incremental rate**: ₱2.65/km beyond 5 km
- **Rounding**: Nearest ₱0.25
- **End-to-end fare**: ~₱74.50 (Monumento↔PITX, ~18 km along EDSA)
- **Legal basis**: LTFRB MC 2017-024; fare matrix published Jan 2023 when Libreng Sakay ended

| Passenger Category | Minimum Fare | Maximum Fare (E2E) |
|--------------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Regular | ₱15.00 | ~₱74.50 |
| Students / SC / PWD (20% disc.) | ₱12.00 | ~₱59.50 |

**Payment**: Cash only as of 2025. Beep card/e-payment integration not yet operational for EDSA Carousel. (Contrast: MRT-3 uses Beep card.)

**Frequency (estimated)**:

- Off-peak headway: likely 5–15 minutes
- Late night (1–4 AM): long waits reported, expect 15–30+ minutes

**Note**: No official published frequency/headway table found. Frequency is demand-managed by the DOTr dispatch system.

## Infrastructure

**Busway**: Innermost lane of EDSA, separated by concrete barriers. Buses use center-island (median) platforms accessed via footbridges, except at PITX (terminal) and several southbound curbside stops in the Pasay/Parañaque area.

**Concourses**: SM Prime inaugurated the first busway concourse in 2024. Bidding for additional concourses at all stations was planned for May 2025, construction target 2025–2027.

**Accessibility**: Elevators/escalators at: Monumento, Bagong Barrio, Balintawak, SM North EDSA, Philam, Guadalupe, One Ayala. Most other stations are footbridge-only (stairs).

**Wi-Fi**: 18 EDSA Carousel stops have free 24/7 WiFi (DICT/DOTr, as of Jan 2026).
