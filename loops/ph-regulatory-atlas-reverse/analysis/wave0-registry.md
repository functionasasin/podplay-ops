## Notes on Coverage Completeness

This registry aimed to be exhaustive. Bodies intentionally excluded from Wave 1 generation:
- **NLRC, COA, CSC, COMELEC, OMB, CHR** — adjudicative or administrative with minimal formula-driven citizen compliance
- **CHED** — tuition regulation requires significant policy judgment, not pure computation
- **DFA passport/apostille** — largely standardized fixed fees, low professional moat
- **DPWH, DND, DOST, DSWD, DepEd** — minimal citizen-facing formula-based compliance
- **NCIP** — FPIC process is judgment-heavy; royalty computation is narrow/specialized
- **SRA, PCA** — narrow agricultural sector, limited computability upside
- **PDIC, NHMFC, SHFC** — either not citizen-facing or already low moat

Wave 1 will scan the 27 generated aspects in detail to surface the most computable domains within each body.
