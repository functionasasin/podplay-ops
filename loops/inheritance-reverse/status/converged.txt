### Key Design Decisions

1. Fully deterministic — no LLM in computation loop
2. Language-agnostic specification
3. Exact rational arithmetic (BigInt fractions)
4. Dual FP tracking (FP_gross for cap base, FP_disposable for testator freedom)
5. Narrative output mandatory — user-targeted engine
6. All ambiguities resolved with citation or flagged as manual review
