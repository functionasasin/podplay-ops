### 3-Year Roadmap Narrative

**Year 1 — Beachhead: Individual Taxpayer & Small Employer**

Launch A1 (self-employed IT) + B3 (compliance calendar) + B-PHI-2 (PhilHealth premium) as a bundled "Freelancer Suite" at ₱199/mo Consumer and ₱999/mo Solo Pro.

Target: 2,500 Consumer subscribers + 300 Solo Pro → ₱6.4M ARR by end of Year 1 (base case).

Acquisition: BIR e-Filing season (March/April); DOLE regional HR seminars; Facebook Groups (freelancer communities); SEO for "BIR 2025 deadline" and "SSS contribution calculator 2025".

**Year 2 — Scale: HR / Payroll Firms & Corporate Secretaries**

Launch full payroll suite (D1+D2+D3+D7+D8) + SEC suite (E1+E2+E4) + VAT engine (C2) targeting CPAs, HR firms, and corporate secretaries.

Introduce Practice tier (₱2,999/mo) with multi-client management. Target accounting firms with 5–50 clients.

Year 2 target: +4,500 Consumer + +1,200 Solo Pro + +200 Practice → cumulative ₱34.4M ARR.

**Year 3 — Expand: Government, OFW, Regulatory Specialty**

Launch GSIS suite (Enterprise), OFW tools (consumer), SEC HARBOR (mandatory corporate), business licensing, and select regulatory specialty tools.

Introduce Partner/Reseller channel: BIR accredited agents who white-label tools for their clients.

Year 3 target: +8,000 Consumer + +2,500 Solo Pro + +60 Practice + +15 Enterprise → cumulative ₱139M ARR (base case).

### Break-Even and Unit Economics

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) assumptions:**
- Consumer: ₱800–₱1,200 (Facebook Ads + content SEO; Philippine B2C SaaS benchmark)
- Solo Pro: ₱3,000–₱5,000 (LinkedIn + PICPA/PMAP partnerships + sales touch)
- Practice: ₱8,000–₱15,000 (direct sales + referral; 2–3 demo calls)
- Enterprise: ₱25,000–₱50,000 (inside sales + custom onboarding)

**Lifetime Value (LTV) assumptions:**
- Consumer: Avg. 8 months retention × ₱199 = **₱1,592 LTV** → LTV/CAC = 1.3–2.0× ⚠️ (thin margin)
- Solo Pro: Avg. 18 months retention × ₱999 = **₱17,982 LTV** → LTV/CAC = 3.6–6.0× ✅
- Practice: Avg. 24 months × ₱2,999 = **₱71,976 LTV** → LTV/CAC = 4.8–9.0× ✅
- Enterprise: Avg. 36 months × ₱9,999 = **₱359,964 LTV** → LTV/CAC = 7.2–14.4× ✅

**Implication:** Consumer tier is a loss leader / awareness funnel. Revenue profitability comes from converting consumers to Solo Pro. **The B2B upward conversion is the core business.**

**Target conversion rate:** 10–15% of Consumer subscribers convert to Solo Pro within 3 months of signup. This is the key metric to optimize.
