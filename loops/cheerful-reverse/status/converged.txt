## Key Architectural Insights

1. ThreadProcessingCoordinatorWorkflow is the beating heart of the system — 20+ AI
   activities orchestrated per inbound email, each with independent retry policies.

2. RAG-enhanced drafting (pgvector + Opus) is the flagship AI feature. Human replies
   become training data that improves every future draft. Style transfer, not just retrieval.

3. Two independent AI systems: backend pipeline (email automation at scale, GPT-4.1
   family + Claude Opus) and context engine (Slack bot for internal team, Claude Opus 4.6
   with meta-tool pattern).

4. Campaign launch is one atomic 13-step database transaction that creates everything:
   campaign, recipients, senders, outbox queue, follow-up queue, and workflows.

5. The system is idempotency-first throughout: ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING everywhere,
   append-only thread state, staleness guards before expensive AI operations.

6. Four automation levels mean the same platform serves fully-manual operators reviewing
   every draft and fully-automated pipelines that never need human touch.

7. Auth is defense-in-depth: JWT at API layer + RLS at DB layer. Backend uses service_role
   (bypasses RLS) but enforces isolation via explicit WHERE user_id = ? clauses. Frontend
   is subject to full RLS.
