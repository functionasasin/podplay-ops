## Feature Inventory (Mapped to Wave 1 Categories)

### Discovery & Search
- **Real-Time Creator Search**: Live audience metrics, engagement trends; filters by platform, reach, location, brand affinity
- **AI Lookalike Creator Search**: Given a top-performing creator, surfaces creators with similar traits, audiences, and performance metrics
- **Marketplace**: 150K opted-in influencers + 120K verified affiliates; curated partner collections; in-line audience quality scores
- **Prospect Import**: Bulk-upload entire prospect lists directly into the platform (bypass manual entry)
- **AI-powered partner recommendations**: Personalizes discovery based on your historical performance

### Outreach & CRM
- **Marketplace-first**: Brands post programs; creators opt in — NOT cold outreach like Cheerful
- **Direct in-platform messaging**: Contracts, negotiations, creative briefs all happen inside impact.com
- **Automated agreement management**: Contracting flow integrated with the payment structure
- **No cold email infrastructure**: Outreach occurs through the marketplace, not user's own email domain

### Campaign Management
- **Unified campaign dashboard**: Manage affiliate + influencer + referral from one interface
- **Flexible commissioning structure**: CPA (% of sale), flat-fee, performance-based hybrid, milestone bonuses
- **Deliverable tracking**: Content deadlines, approval status
- **Brief delivery**: Via in-platform messaging and Media Vault uploads

### Content Collaboration
- **Media Vault**: Auto-captures all creator-submitted content per campaign; searchable with custom tags and identifiers
- **Usage rights management**: Rights terms baked into contracting, explicitly tracked per asset
- **UGC repurposing library**: Brands can pull approved content for ads, social posts, etc.

### Payments & Contracts
- **Automated payout engine**: Commission, flat-fee, or hybrid; configurable per creator per program
- **Automatic commission reversal**: Shopify-integrated; when a buyer returns an order, creator commission auto-adjusts — no manual correction
- **Multi-payout method support**: Wire, PayPal, Payoneer (varies by region)
- **Fraud protection**: Built-in fraud detection on transactions
- **Annual 1099 support**: For US-based affiliate/creator tax compliance

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Cookieless full-funnel attribution**: Tracks first click → conversion → customer lifetime value (no browser cookies required)
- **Multi-channel attribution deduplication**: Deduplicate attribution across affiliate, influencer, paid search, social, direct — assigns credit fairly
- **Partner ROI reporting**: CPA, CLV, total clicks, total conversions, incremental impact per partner
- **Granular customer journey reports**: Full path from discovery through purchase
- **Social Listening**: Real-time brand/competitor/partner monitoring across social platforms

### Integrations & Ecosystem
- **45+ direct integrations**: Shopify (deep), BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Recurly, Adjust (mobile attribution), Supermetrics, and more
- **REST API**: Full API for automation, custom tracking, and data export
- **Shopify integration**: Auto-sync orders, commission calculation, reversal for returns — without developer work
- **Salesforce integration**: Map conversions to CRM opportunities; track partner-to-sales-pipeline attribution
- **HubSpot integration**: Marketing data sync

### AI & Automation
- **Ask Impact AI**: Conversational in-platform AI assistant — draws from user analytics, help center, and Seismic library; acts as analyst + trainer + assistant
- **Lookalike creator AI**: Automatically surfaces similar creators to your best performers
- **Predictive benchmarking**: Benchmark data for new partner discovery opportunities

### Marketplace & Network
- **Partnership Cloud marketplace**: 120K verified affiliates + 150K opted-in influencers; the largest multi-type partner network in the industry
- **Curated collections**: Pre-vetted creator groups by category, audience size, platform

### Team Collaboration
- **Program-level access controls**: Role-based access for brand teams
- **Approval workflows**: Content approval baked into Media Vault submission flow
- **Multiple brand/program management**: Enterprises run many programs simultaneously

## Unique Differentiators

1. **Affiliate + Influencer Unified** — The only enterprise platform that fully merges affiliate (commission, tracking links) with influencer (content, relationships) under one tracking system. Brands running both don't need two tools.

2. **Cookieless Full-Funnel Attribution** — Proprietary cookieless tracking that follows the customer from first creator touchpoint to purchase to repeat purchase. Far superior to pixel-based tracking that breaks with iOS/browser privacy changes.

3. **Automatic Commission Reversal** — When a Shopify customer returns an order, impact.com auto-reverts the affiliate/creator commission. No other influencer platform handles this edge case automatically. Prevents over-paying creators for returned orders.

4. **Flexible Per-Partner Commissioning** — Each creator can have a unique deal structure: flat-fee only, commission only, or flat fee + % of revenue. This is normal in affiliate marketing but rare in influencer platforms, which default to flat-fee-only.

5. **Ask Impact AI** — A conversational in-platform AI that knows your own program data, can answer questions like "which creator category drives the highest LTV customers," and guides strategy — not just generates content.

6. **Media Vault with Rights Management** — Every piece of creator-submitted content is auto-saved, tagged, and linked to explicit usage rights terms. Brands can confidently repurpose UGC for paid ads.

## Workflow Integration Depth

**Very high for affiliate-first teams**: impact.com becomes the payment processor, contract repository, tracking layer, and attribution source — all in one. Exiting means rebuilding all of this.

**Moderate for influencer-first teams**: Brands typically adopt impact.com for their affiliate program and add influencers as a secondary use case. For teams doing influencer-only campaigns, it's over-engineered and expensive.

**Key daily touchpoints**: Partner discovery → contract management → content approval → payment processing → attribution reporting. Teams that use all five are deeply locked in.

## What Cheerful Can Learn

### 1. Hybrid Commission + Flat-Fee Creator Pay
impact.com normalizes giving each creator a custom deal structure. Cheerful's `paid_promotion_rate` captures rate data from emails — but brands still manually send money outside the platform. Cheerful should close this loop: let brands define a creator's deal as "flat fee + 5% of sales" and handle payout automatically through Stripe Connect. This is the highest-stickiness feature gap — once brands route payments through Cheerful, switching is catastrophic.

### 2. Lookalike Creator Discovery (Already Partially Planned)
impact.com's AI surfaces creators similar to your best performers. Cheerful ALREADY HAS a `campaign_lookalike_suggestion` table in its schema — this feature is architecturally anticipated. Completing it would create a powerful retention driver: once a brand has run 5+ campaigns through Cheerful, the system knows their best-performing creator profiles and can surface lookalikes automatically. Competitors can't replicate this without your historical campaign + engagement data.

### 3. UTM + Promo Code Revenue Attribution Bridge
impact.com's core value prop is knowing which partner drove which dollar. Cheerful should generate unique UTM-tagged links and promo codes per creator per campaign (synced to Shopify via direct API, not GoAffPro). Track clicks → orders → revenue. Show brands a creator's true ROAS (return on ad spend), not just their engagement rate. This would be a tier-1 feature that no mid-market influencer platform has natively built as well as impact.com — and Cheerful's deep Shopify knowledge from GoAffPro is a starting point.

## Top 3 Hero Feature Candidates

### Hero Feature A: Hybrid Commission + Flat-Fee Creator Pay Hub
**One-line pitch**: Let brands pay creators a flat fee + % of sales — all tracked and disbursed through Cheerful.
**Stickiness rationale**: Financial records are the stickiest data possible. Once a brand's creator payment history lives in Cheerful, migrating means convincing finance, legal, and ops teams to rebuild payout infrastructure — 6–18 months of switching cost.
**Builds on**: `paid_promotion_rate`, `campaign_creator`, GoAffPro Shopify order data; needs Stripe Connect for disbursement.
**Cheerful advantage vs. impact.com**: impact.com is marketplace-inbound only; Cheerful's Gmail-native outreach + pay engine creates a complete cycle (recruit cold → manage → pay) that impact.com cannot replicate.

### Hero Feature B: Lookalike Creator Engine
**One-line pitch**: After a successful campaign, automatically surface 10 similar creators ranked by predicted performance.
**Stickiness rationale**: The engine gets smarter with every campaign run in Cheerful — it accumulates brand-specific performance fingerprints. Switching means losing the predictive model trained on your specific brand's history.
**Builds on**: `campaign_lookalike_suggestion` table (already in schema), `creator` profiles, `campaign_creator` performance data.
**Cheerful advantage vs. impact.com**: impact.com's lookalike is based on profile similarity only; Cheerful's can incorporate reply rate, negotiation success rate, post performance — behavioral signals impact.com can't access because they don't do cold outreach.

### Hero Feature C: Creator Revenue Attribution Bridge (UTM + Promo → Shopify)
**One-line pitch**: Auto-generate unique UTM links and promo codes per creator, track all the way to Shopify revenue, show true ROAS per creator.
**Stickiness rationale**: Revenue attribution data becomes the primary signal for creator re-booking decisions. Once teams use this for 6 months, campaign planning is impossible without it — it answers "which creator is worth paying again?"
**Builds on**: Shopify GoAffPro integration, `creator_post` table, `campaign_creator` table; needs Shopify Admin API upgrade from GoAffPro to direct OAuth.
**Cheerful advantage vs. impact.com**: Cheerful manages the outreach conversation AND can track performance — end-to-end within one platform. impact.com has no outreach capability; Cheerful would be the only platform with the full picture from cold email to sale.
