## Feature Inventory (Mapped to Wave 1 Categories)

### Discovery & Search
- **68.7M+ creator profiles** (some sources cite 206.8M+) across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X, Twitch
- AI-powered natural language search; keyword + niche + geographic filters; 253 countries
- **Lookalike finder** — identifies creators with similar performance and audience to a given creator
- **Influential Customers** — syncs e-commerce customer list to identify shoppers who are also creators
- **Social Listening** — monitors brand mentions to surface organic brand advocates and potential ambassadors
- **Suggester API** — find creators by name/handle across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X, Twitch

### Fraud Detection (Industry-Leading)
- **35 audience quality metrics** including follower credibility, comment authenticity, growth pattern anomaly
- **95.5% fraud detection accuracy** (industry-leading; closest competitor is CreatorIQ's 40-language model)
- Audience Authenticity Score + Quality Score per creator
- Audience Overlap analysis (avoid booking two creators whose audiences heavily overlap)

### Audience Analytics
- Deep demographic breakdown: country, city, age, gender, language, **ethnicity** — per-creator
- Audience credibility segmentation (real followers vs. suspects vs. mass followers)
- **Audience interests** and **brand affinity** signals
- Cross-platform audience overlap visualization

### Outreach / CRM
- **Bulk email outreach** from the platform
- Email stored centrally — **NOT sent from brand's own Gmail domain**
- No AI personalization engine (generic templates)
- No inbox sync or reply management within the platform

### Campaign Management
- Branded media plans / campaign briefs with deliverable scheduling
- Campaign performance tracking vs. targets
- Content approval workflow (submit → review → approve)
- Cannot track Instagram/TikTok **Stories** — ephemeral content entirely missed

### Payments
- In-platform payments capability (scope unclear from public sources)
- No confirmed Stripe/Tipalti/wire integration at SMB tier

### Analytics & Reporting
- Per-creator engagement, reach, EMV, and post-level performance
- **Competitor Analysis** — track competitors' influencer rosters and strategies
- **Competitor Comparison** — side-by-side brand vs. brand influencer share-of-voice
- Market Analysis reports by country/niche/platform

### E-Commerce Integrations
- **Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento** — native integrations
- Affiliate link creation and promo code distribution
- **JS sales tracking snippet** (March 2025) — tracks clicks, orders, total sales on any storefront (not just Shopify)
- Product seeding / gifting management (via e-commerce integrations)

### Ambassador Programs
- **Ambassador Landing Pages** — branded custom URLs and lead-capture forms for inbound creator recruitment
- Product seeding at scale to ambassador cohorts

### API
- Public API available (add-on tier, $55K+/yr equivalent implied)
- Enables custom database queries, benchmarking, and integration with in-house tools

## Unique Differentiators

1. **Best-in-class fraud detection for the mid-market** — 35 metrics, 95.5% accuracy, Audience Authenticity Score. No SMB competitor comes close. Closest is CreatorIQ (enterprise, 10x price) or modash (simpler model).

2. **Audience overlap analysis** — prevents booking two creators who reach the same people; unique in the SMB tier. Critical for agencies running multi-creator campaigns.

3. **Competitor influencer analysis** — lets brands track which creators competitors are using, their share-of-voice, and campaign strategies. Only Traackr and CreatorIQ offer similar at much higher price.

4. **Social Listening → Creator Sourcing pipeline** — automatically surfaces creators already organically talking about the brand; reduces cold outreach friction.

5. **Influential Customers** feature — converts own customer database into a warm creator discovery source (similar to GRIN and Upfluence, but in the fraud-detection specialist wrapper).

6. **JS sales tracking (March 2025)** — platform-agnostic click-to-sale attribution without Shopify dependency; first-mover advantage in this mechanism at this price tier.

7. **Ambassador Landing Pages** — enables inbound creator pipeline via branded sign-up pages; turn paid media or email into creator recruitment funnel.

## Workflow Integration Depth

**Current depth: Medium-Low**

HypeAuditor's deepest workflow touchpoints are:
- Discovery and vetting (fraud scores embedded in every creator profile)
- Campaign brief creation (branded media plans)
- E-commerce tracking (Shopify/WooCommerce/JS snippet → sales data in-platform)

**Workflow gaps that limit stickiness:**
- No Gmail-native email → operators manage outreach replies outside HypeAuditor
- No Story tracking → teams use separate tools (archive.ph, AspireIQ) for ephemeral content
- No robust multi-user collaboration → agencies duplicate work across accounts
- No CRM or creator history → cross-campaign relationship context is lost

**Stickiness rating: 3/5** — The fraud detection + competitor analysis creates genuine data-accumulation lock-in (you build historical benchmarks over time), but the workflow gaps push teams to use it alongside other tools rather than exclusively.

## What Cheerful Can Learn

### High-Priority Borrowing Opportunities

**1. Audience Quality Score / Creator Shield**
HypeAuditor's 35-metric fraud model is the clearest precedent for a "Creator Quality Shield" in Cheerful. Cheerful currently has zero fraud scoring. Even a simplified 5-metric authenticity index (follower growth velocity, engagement rate vs. benchmark, comment-to-like ratio, mass-follower percentage, account age) would close this gap.
- *Cheerful spec tie-in:* `creator` table has `follower_count`, `following_count`, `engagement_rate` — foundation exists; needs expanded metric calculation layer

**2. Competitor Influencer Intelligence**
Brands desperately want to know: "Which creators are my competitors using, and how is that performing?" HypeAuditor and Traackr offer this; no SMB-tier competitor does it well.
- *Cheerful spec tie-in:* Cheerful has `campaign_mention_brand` table for extracted brand mentions. With a "Watched Brands" feature + periodic search automation via Apify, Cheerful could build a basic competitor roster tracker without licensing external data.

**3. Ambassador Landing Pages**
Custom-URL inbound creator recruitment pages (e.g., `brand.cheeful.co/join`) that capture creator applications and automatically populate the creator database. HypeAuditor makes this turnkey; Cheerful has zero inbound recruitment infrastructure.
- *Cheerful spec tie-in:* Would need new `creator_application` table + public-facing form page; backend API already handles creator records

**4. Audience Overlap Detection**
When a user builds a campaign roster with 5+ creators, show audience overlap percentage between them. Cheerful currently books creators independently with no deduplication of audience reach — brands unknowingly buy the same eyes multiple times.
- *Cheerful spec tie-in:* No current support; would require audience demographic storage (not currently captured) or third-party data enrichment

**5. JS Sales Tracking Snippet**
HypeAuditor's March 2025 launch of a JS snippet for non-Shopify storefronts is clever — it extends attribution tracking beyond the Shopify ecosystem to any web store. Cheerful currently relies on GoAffPro (Shopify-only), missing WooCommerce, Magento, and custom stores.
- *Cheerful spec tie-in:* Would extend beyond current GoAffPro integration

### 3 Hero Feature Candidates

| # | Feature | What HypeAuditor Proves | Cheerful Advantage |
|---|---------|------------------------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Creator Quality Shield** — authenticity score, fraud flag, engagement benchmark per creator | 95.5% fraud accuracy = table stakes for mid-market | Cheerful already captures `engagement_rate` + growth data via Apify; can build scoring on top |
| 2 | **Competitor Roster Intelligence** — track which creators competitors are using, their SOV, and campaign frequency | Traackr charges $8K/yr for benchmarking add-on; HypeAuditor includes at lower tier | Cheerful's outbound-first model can passively build this via search pattern reuse + `campaign_mention_brand` LLM extraction |
| 3 | **Audience Overlap Guard** — detect when campaign roster creators share too much audience before booking | No SMB competitor has this in the roster-building flow | Could surface at roster review step in campaign wizard (before outreach is sent), preventing waste |

## Competitive Position Summary

| Dimension | HypeAuditor | Cheerful |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| Fraud detection | Best-in-class (35 metrics) | None |
| Creator database | 68.7M–206M profiles | On-demand scrape (unlimited but slow 20–120s) |
| Email outreach | Platform-hosted (reply-rate disadvantage) | Gmail-native (decisive moat) |
| Audience analytics | Deep (demographics, ethnicity, overlap) | Minimal (basic follower counts) |
| Competitor intelligence | Yes (brand SOV, roster tracking) | None |
| Shopify integration | Yes (affiliate links, promo codes) | Shallow (GoAffPro gifting only) |
| Story tracking | No | No (parity) |
| Multi-user collaboration | Weak | Moderate (teams, RLS, roles) |
| Pricing | $2,999+/yr, annual lock-in | SMB-accessible, no annual lock-in |
| Ambassador landing pages | Yes | None |
| Billing reputation | Poor (Trustpilot 2025–2026) | N/A |

**Summary:** HypeAuditor's moat is fraud detection depth + competitor intelligence + audience overlap analytics — capabilities Cheerful entirely lacks. However, HypeAuditor's platform-hosted email is a fundamental disadvantage vs. Cheerful's Gmail-native delivery, and their billing/cancellation practices are actively damaging their SMB reputation (opportunity for Cheerful to win churned customers). The Creator Quality Shield, Competitor Roster Intelligence, and Audience Overlap Guard are the three features Cheerful must prioritize to close the most visible gap.
