## Feature Inventory (Mapped to Wave 1 Categories)

### Discovery & Search
- **Content-first AI indexing:** Analyzes image content, location, mentions, emojis — not just bio/caption keywords — across 1 billion+ public social accounts
- **Multi-platform:** Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Pinterest — all via direct API partnerships (not scraping)
- **20 million creator private network** (brand-owned lists) + broader indexed universe
- **SafeIQ pre-vetting:** Creators surface already tagged for brand safety/suitability
- **Google AI image recognition:** Detects creator interests and brand affinities from visual content

**Note:** Despite the depth, user reviews flag that discovery "is not always accurate and recommends influencers that don't match filters." Cross-platform profile linking is also weak — profiles aren't connected across platforms.

### Outreach & CRM
- Full **Influencer CRM** for relationship tracking, campaign history, communication logs
- **Creator Campaign Portal:** Branded application pages where creators can apply to campaigns
- Ongoing relationship health tracking across programs (not just per-campaign)

### Campaign Management
- End-to-end campaign lifecycle management
- Configurable approval workflows
- 44K+ campaigns managed in 2025 across 170 countries

### Content Collaboration
- Content review and approval queue built into campaign workflows
- Brief delivery and content submission via creator portal

### Payments & Contracts
- **CreatorIQ Pay:** Integrated global payment system
  - Pays in **60+ currencies across 80+ markets**
  - Fund accounts in **22 currencies**
  - Local-to-local payments to cut FX fees
  - Automated creator onboarding (self-service setup, reminders)
  - Role-based payment dashboards (campaign managers + finance teams)
  - Audit-ready compliance controls
- Creator payment growth: **+79% YoY in 2025**
- User review: "CreatorIQ has completely streamlined [payments] allowing us to pay creators quickly and efficiently without the usual red tape."

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Standardized Metrics Suite** (launched May 2025): Industry-first standardized benchmarks
  - Share of Voice (SOV) — content volume / brand presence
  - Share of Exposure (SOX) — estimated impressions / visibility
  - Share of Engagement (SOE) — audience interactions / resonance
  - Share of Influence (SOI) — earned media value / business impact
- **BenchmarkIQ:** Competitive benchmarking across 37 countries, 18 industries, 1.2M creator panel
  - Weekly data refresh; track competitive, complementary, or aspirational brands
  - Multi-market dashboards with flexible filters (country, creator tier, content type, platform)
  - Top 100 creators and content tracking per competitor
- Real-time, customizable reporting dashboards
- White-label reporting for agency clients
- **Creator Marketing Industry Benchmarks Calculator** — free, open-access (public marketing move)

### Integrations & Ecosystem
- **ExchangeIQ API:** Custom enterprise API for connecting creator data to CRMs, BI tools, data lakes
  - JSON-based, SSL/TLS encrypted
  - Full API documentation at apidocs.creatoriq.com
- **Social platform API partnerships:** Meta, Snapchat, TikTok, YouTube (direct API, not scraping)
- **SSO** support for enterprise authentication
- Configurable enterprise workflows and integrations
- **ISO 27001 certified** enterprise security

### AI & Automation
- **Creator Graph:** Proprietary intelligence engine
  - Processes **123 million social media posts daily**
  - Trained on 10+ years of creator, content, and performance data
  - Identifies a panel of 1.1 million influential creators for benchmarking
- **SafeIQ** (launched October 21, 2025): Industry-first AI-native brand safety
  - Multimodal: analyzes text, images, video, and audio across languages
  - Self-learning: continuously adapts to each brand's unique suitability thresholds (not static keyword filters)
  - Proactive: detects risk before campaigns launch (vs. reactive discovery after publication)
  - Efficiency: reviews a 40-minute video in under 1 minute
  - Multi-language + cultural nuance detection
  - Available standalone (even for non-CreatorIQ customers — strategic market move)
  - **State of Safety Report:** 74% of enterprise marketers say brand safety became more critical in 2025; 82% of brands prioritizing safety saw higher ROI

### Marketplace & Network
- Branded creator application pages — inbound creator applications to brand programs
- Private creator network management (brand-owned rosters)

### Team Collaboration
- Role-based permissions across teams (campaign managers, finance, executives)
- Shared environments for cross-org collaboration (e.g., brand + agency)
- Audit-ready workflows for regulated industries

## Unique Differentiators

1. **Creator Graph™** — A decade of proprietary intelligence that no competitor can replicate overnight. Processes 123M posts/day, tracks long-term creator/content/performance trajectories. This is genuine compounding data moat.

2. **BenchmarkIQ Share-of-Voice Suite** — Industry's first standardized competitive benchmarking for creator marketing. SOV/SOX/SOE/SOI framework with 37-country coverage creates a measurement language the industry is now adopting as a standard.

3. **SafeIQ** — Proactive AI brand safety that learns each brand's unique standards. Not a keyword filter — a self-learning multimodal system. Offered standalone (outside CreatorIQ) as a land-and-expand strategy.

4. **CreatorIQ Pay** — 80+ markets, 60+ currencies, integrated into campaign workflow. Finance and campaign teams share a payment dashboard. The +79% YoY growth signals this is a core stickiness feature.

5. **Fully integrated operating system vision** — Paid + owned + earned + commerce + community in one platform. The "operating system" framing is deliberate and defines the land-and-expand strategy.

## What Cheerful Can Learn

### 1. SafeIQ — Proactive Brand Safety (Build from scratch)
**Biggest gap:** Cheerful has zero brand safety infrastructure. SafeIQ is a hero feature candidate.
- Multimodal analysis of creator content history before outreach
- Self-learning threshold per campaign/client (not a static blocklist)
- "Reviewer" mode: surface past creator content that may conflict with brand values
- Cross-reference with the `creator_profile` table in Cheerful — brand safety score could be a first-class field
- **Stickiness:** Once brands tune their safety thresholds, switching means losing all calibration data

### 2. BenchmarkIQ — Competitive Share-of-Voice Analytics (Build from scratch)
**Biggest gap:** Cheerful has no competitive benchmarking. Brands flying blind on market position.
- Track client's creator content volume vs. 3 named competitors in the same industry
- Expose SOV/SOE metrics in the analytics dashboard
- **Note:** SOV data requires industry panels — could start with client's own portfolio benchmarking (cross-campaign performance comparisons) before building competitive data
- **Stickiness:** Reports become embedded in executive decks; switching means rebuilding historical baselines

### 3. Creator Graph Concept — Longitudinal Creator Intelligence (Enhance existing)
Cheerful currently does on-demand Apify scraping (no historical data). The Creator Graph idea: **track creator metrics over time** (follower growth, engagement trends, posting frequency, collaboration history). Even at a small scale (creators a brand has worked with), this accumulating dataset is highly sticky.
- Enhance `creator_profile` with time-series metrics table
- Track every Cheerful brand's creator relationships across campaigns — build brand-specific creator ROI history
- **Stickiness:** After 12 months of data, the dataset is irreplaceable

### 4. Creator Payment + Finance Dashboard (Build from scratch — previously identified)
Already noted in Wave 1 `payments-contracts.md`. CreatorIQ's +79% YoY payment growth confirms this is where revenue concentration and stickiness live. The finance dashboard concept — campaign managers approve, finance sees all transactions — is the key workflow innovation. Separate from just "send money," it's about role-specific views.

### 5. Branded Creator Application Portal (Build from scratch)
Creators apply to campaigns on a branded page. This flips outreach from 100% cold to partially inbound. Already noted as a hero candidate in `marketplace-network.md` and `campaign-management.md`. CreatorIQ's success validates the model at enterprise scale.

### 6. White-Label Agency Reports (Enhance existing)
Cheerful has Google Sheets LLM-export metrics. CreatorIQ's white-label reports are a client-retention mechanism for agencies. Cheerful's Google Sheets exports could be elevated to branded PDF/web reports — a single feature that makes every Cheerful agency customer's client relationship stickier.

## Hero Feature Candidates Surfaced

1. **Proactive Brand Safety Shield (SafeIQ-inspired)** — AI content audit of creators before outreach, with per-campaign customizable thresholds and self-learning from brand rejections. Stickiness driver: calibration data becomes irreplaceable over time.

2. **Longitudinal Creator Intelligence Layer (Creator Graph-inspired)** — Time-series tracking of creator metrics for every creator in Cheerful's database. Turns Cheerful's transactional discovery into a compounding intelligence asset. Stickiness driver: data accumulation makes the roster more valuable the longer brands stay.

3. **Competitive Share-of-Voice Dashboard (BenchmarkIQ-inspired)** — Let brands compare their creator marketing investment vs. competitor spend/exposure. Even a simplified SOV metric would be a differentiated feature in the mid-market where no competitor offers this below enterprise pricing ($30K+/year). Stickiness driver: executive reporting dependency.
