## Feature Inventory (Mapped to Wave 1 Categories)

### Discovery & Search
- **Database:** ~30M influencer profiles (significantly smaller than CreatorIQ 250M+ or Sprout Social 250M+)
- **Proprietary "Influence Score"**: Composite metric beyond follower count — evaluates audience quality, content relevance, engagement authenticity, brand affinity, posting consistency, and historical performance
- **"True Reach" metric**: Differentiates real reach from inflated follower counts — praised as "groundbreaking" by reviewers
- **AI-powered search**: Text, topic, and image-based search with advanced filters
- **Filters**: Category, geographic location, nano/micro/mega tier, platform
- **Branded Recruitment Pages**: Custom application pages that attract inbound creators

**Weakness:** Database (~30M) is 8x smaller than competitors like CreatorIQ or Sprout Social. Nano/local influencer support is weak — many profiles lack audience demographic data. Mandatory influencer authentication creates onboarding friction.

### Outreach & CRM (Klear Connect)
- **Klear Connect Portal**: Dedicated influencer collaboration hub — campaign brief sending, content approval, payment, contract signing
- **Centralized inbox**: Consolidates personal outreach, mass messaging, asset distribution, campaign conversations
- **Email integration**: Campaign messaging via simple email integration that populates within platform
- **Relationship management**: "Enterprise-grade CRM" covering conversations, content collaboration, timelines

### Campaign Management
- **End-to-end campaign lifecycle**: Outreach → collaboration → content review → contracting → payments → performance monitoring → competitive benchmarking — all in one environment
- **Campaign timelines**: Built-in timeline management
- **Content approval workflows**: In-platform review and approval

### Content Collaboration
- **Content review and approval in Klear Connect**
- **Asset distribution** through centralized inbox
- **UGC tracking**: Platform captures campaign content (with noted gaps for Stories and ephemeral content)

**Weakness:** "Sometimes does not gather all the contents of a campaign" — reviewers note manual content additions needed.

### Payments & Contracts
- **In-app contracts**: Built directly into workflow
- **Global payments**: Nearly 100 countries with local currency support (powered by third-party Tipalti)
- **E-commerce coupon codes**: Shopify integration for measuring influencer-driven revenue
- **Tipalti payment infrastructure**: Handles payment compliance globally

**Weakness:** Tipalti integration is "very in depth and complicated to set up" — requires extensive financial documentation that some companies cannot legally provide. Multiple reviewers paid influencers *outside* the platform due to setup friction.

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Real-time campaign analytics**
- **ROI measurement**: Claimed 307% average ROI increase, $939K cost reduction, 80%+ efficiency gains (marketing claims)
- **Competitive benchmarking**: Measures influencer performance against competitors and leading brands
- **Google Sheets export** (noted with limitations)
- **Audience demographic data**: Geo, age, gender breakdowns

**Weakness:** "Does not offer more specific data" — global metrics shown rather than campaign-specific. Reporting accuracy issues (e.g., shows US audience when actual audience is Costa Rica). Export doesn't pull in all images/graphs. Limited historical data access.

### Integrations & Ecosystem
- **Social Suite (Engage)**: Unified social listening + social management + influencer marketing
- **Klear ↔ Engage integration** (May 2024): Discover influencer posts → amplify on owned channels; unified influencer + owned social dashboard
- **Shopify**: Revenue attribution, coupon codes, gifting
- **Tipalti**: Global payment processing
- **Social listening**: Media intelligence monitoring integrated with influencer performance
- **Consumer intelligence**: Brand sentiment + competitive intelligence alongside influencer campaign monitoring

**Notable differentiation:** Meltwater is the only platform that combines PR media monitoring + social listening + social publishing + influencer marketing in a single suite. This cross-channel intelligence is unique.

### AI & Automation
- **AI Discovery (roadmap)**: Next-gen autonomous influencer search
- **AI Outreach (roadmap)**: Autonomous personalized outreach
- **AI-powered summaries**: Help determine influencer-brand fit
- **Influence Score algorithm**: Proprietary composite metric
- **1 billion+ content pieces/day** processed for AI training

### Marketplace & Network
- No explicit marketplace/opt-in creator network mentioned
- Relies primarily on third-party database + proprietary data enrichment

### Team Collaboration
- **Workspaces**: Multi-team support mentioned in documentation
- **Centralized inbox**: Designed for large-scale coordination
- Specific role/permission details not publicly documented

## Unique Differentiators

1. **Only true PR + influencer suite**: Media monitoring, social listening, social publishing, AND influencer marketing in one platform — no competitor combines all four
2. **Influence Score**: Composite creator quality metric beyond basic engagement rates
3. **True Reach**: Validated reach metric distinguishing real vs. inflated audiences
4. **Competitive benchmarking built-in**: Compare your influencer program vs. competitors — rare feature at this depth
5. **IDC dual Leader recognition**: Recognized for both enterprise AND SMB segments simultaneously (unique distinction)
6. **Near-100-country payment coverage**: Global payment reach rivals enterprise-only competitors
7. **Cross-channel amplification**: Klear ↔ Engage integration lets teams repurpose influencer content on owned channels seamlessly

## Workflow Integration Depth

**Very high** — Meltwater is designed as a cross-functional platform that bridges multiple teams:

1. **PR teams**: Media monitoring → influencer identification → campaign launch
2. **Social teams**: Social listening → influencer amplification → owned channel publishing
3. **Analytics teams**: Campaign performance + competitive benchmarking in one view
4. **Finance teams**: Global payment processing through Tipalti

The **suite integration** (Klear + Engage + media monitoring) creates significant switching costs because migrating means losing the cross-channel intelligence layer that no point solution replicates.

## What Cheerful Can Learn

### Feature Gaps to Address

1. **Competitive benchmarking**: Meltwater lets brands compare their influencer program against competitors' influencer programs. Cheerful has no competitive intelligence layer. Adding competitive benchmarking to analytics dashboards would be highly differentiating for agency clients managing multiple brands. *(Reference: `spec-backend-api.md` — analytics endpoints exist but no competitive data ingestion)*

2. **Cross-channel amplification workflow**: Meltwater's Klear ↔ Engage integration lets teams discover influencer posts and immediately share/amplify them on owned social channels. Cheerful's scope is currently influencer-only; adding owned social publishing as an amplification layer would make the platform stickier. *(Reference: `spec-integrations.md` — no owned social publishing integrations)*

3. **Proprietary creator quality score**: Meltwater's Influence Score is a composite metric that combines 6+ signals. Cheerful has standard engagement + fraud detection but no branded composite score. A proprietary "Cheerful Creator Score" would differentiate discovery and build trust with buyers. *(Reference: `spec-data-model.md` — creator profile tables exist but no composite scoring)*

4. **Branded recruitment pages**: Meltwater lets brands publish custom creator application pages. Cheerful has outbound-only discovery; adding inbound creator applications via branded pages would flip the model and reduce sourcing costs. *(Reference: `spec-webapp.md` — no inbound application flows)*

5. **Payment complexity is the gap to exploit**: Tipalti-powered payments in Meltwater are notoriously hard to set up. Cheerful's current payment integration via GoAffPro/Shopify is narrow. A simpler, more accessible global payment solution (e.g., Stripe Connect or Wise) built natively into Cheerful's workflow could steal deals from Meltwater where payment friction is the blocker.

### Approaches Worth Adopting

- **Suite narrative**: Meltwater wins deals by being "one platform for everything." Cheerful should develop a clear "operating system" narrative even before full feature parity — the story matters as much as the capability.
- **Influence Score branding**: Proprietary metrics become product moats. Name and brand creator quality assessments.
- **Competitive reporting**: Show clients how their influencer program compares to competitors — turns Cheerful into a strategic intelligence tool, not just an ops tool.
