## Category-by-Category Breakdown

### 1. Discovery & Search (DS)

**Cheerful: ⚡ Partial** — on-demand Apify scraping for Instagram + YouTube only; 20–180s latency; no pre-indexed database; no TikTok; no fraud scoring; no audience demographic filters.

| Leader | What Makes Them Best-in-Class |
|--------|-------------------------------|
| Modash | 350M+ pre-indexed profiles; AI image search; zero-signup; audience overlap; sub-second results |
| HypeAuditor | 35-metric fraud detection at 98.3% accuracy; 200+ data points per creator; SMB price |
| Influencer Hero | 450M+ profiles, 8 platforms, natural language "describe your ideal creator" search |
| CreatorIQ | 123M posts/day ingested; longitudinal Creator Graph; predictive AI recommendations; enterprise-grade |
| Sprout Social | 250M+ profiles; Brand Fit Score AI; YouTube BrandConnect exclusive; semantic topic search |
| Captiv8 | 500+ search filters (industry's most); DEI ethnicity/orientation filters; Snapchat official API |

**Cheerful gap**: No TikTok, no pre-indexed database, no fraud detection, no audience demographic filters. Cheerful's discovery is 10–100× slower than competitors. **Discovery is the #1 workflow entry point** — owning it is owning the whole workflow.

---

### 2. Outreach / CRM (OR)

**Cheerful: ✅ Best-in-class (Gmail-native outreach layer)** — Temporal-powered email sequences, 60+ Gmail accounts, Claude claude-opus-4-6 AI personalization, RAG-based reply drafts, BulkDraftEditWorkflow. This is Cheerful's single biggest competitive moat.

| Platform | Outreach Architecture | Critical Weakness |
|----------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Cheerful | Gmail-native (brand's own domain) | No kanban CRM pipeline, no creator notes, no cross-campaign relationship view |
| Modash | Gmail-native (shared moat) | No AI personalization, no drip sequences |
| GRIN | Platform email domain | Reply-rate disadvantage vs Cheerful; persistent bugs |
| Upfluence | Upfluence domain email | "Next to none of automated email sequencing" (G2); major reply-rate gap |
| Sprout Social | Sprout domain email | "Emails sent from Sprout's domain NOT user's Gmail" — explicit G2 complaint |
| Aspire | Aspire domain email | Same fundamental deliverability disadvantage |
| Later Influence | Later domain email | Same disadvantage |
| Impact.com | Marketplace-only | Cannot cold-email creators at all |
| Linqia | Managed service | Cannot cold-email creators at all |

**Cheerful advantage**: Only Cheerful and Modash send outreach from the **brand's own Gmail domain**. Every other competitor routes through their platform's servers — delivering measurably lower reply rates. Cheerful's Temporal automation layer is far deeper than Modash's.

**Cheerful gaps**: No visual pipeline/kanban CRM view, no cross-campaign creator history, no contact notes, no creator onboarding portal.

### 5. Payments & Contracts (PC)

**Cheerful: ❌ None** — The single biggest capability gap. Full 8-stage `paid_promotion_status` machine exists (LLM-extracted from email) but zero payment disbursement, contracts, e-signature, W-9/W-8, or 1099 infrastructure.

| Platform | Payment & Contract Capability |
|----------|-------------------------------|
| CreatorIQ Pay | 80+ markets, 60+ currencies, +79% YoY; full compliance |
| Traackr | 215+ countries, Fee Recommendation Engine, 2.5yr rate history |
| GRIN | DocuSign embedded, 1099 generation, Klaviyo-gated gifting flow |
| Upfluence Pay | W-9/W-8 auto-collected, tax reporting, global |
| Modash | 180+ countries, 36 currencies, global payments built-in |
| Collabstr | Escrow via Dots infrastructure, 100+ countries, dispute protection |
| Later/Mavely | One-click affiliate links, 9 affiliate networks, automated commissions |
| Aspire | Contracts, payments, zero creator commission |

**Critical facts**:
- IRS penalties: $50–$270 per missing W-9 ($135K fine for 500-creator program)
- FTC fined brands $112M in 2025 for inadequate contract disclosures
- Lumanu processes $1M+/day — payment lock-in is the deepest stickiness driver in the entire market
- Cheerful's `PAID` status is honor-system only — teams must exit to DocuSign + wire for every deal

**Priority**: Highest-stickiness gap to close. Once Cheerful holds financial audit trails + tax records, migration becomes an 18-month reconstruction project.

| Platform | Signature Analytics Feature |

| Archive | EMV calculation, competitor creator intelligence, brand mention quantification |
| Brand24 | Presence Score, Reputation Score, SOV shift tracking |
| Impact.com | Cookieless full-funnel attribution (click → sale → LTV); Shopify return auto-deduction |
| GRIN + Upfluence | Per-creator Shopify revenue attribution, promo code tracking |
| Captiv8 | Perplexity AI auto-generated wrap reports (narrative + insights in minutes) |

**Cheerful gap**: 82% of brands now prioritize ROI tracking above follower counts (2026 data). Avg campaign ROI is $5.20/$1 invested, best-tracked campaigns reach $18–20. Cheerful cannot produce a revenue attribution number — the most critical metric in 2026.

| Pre-launch performance predictor | CreatorIQ, Traackr, GRIN Gia | ❌ Not built |

### 9. Marketplace / Network (MN)

**Cheerful: ❌ None** — 100% outbound-only. No inbound creator application flow, no creator opt-in network, no ambassador tiers, no reputation layer.

| Marketplace Leader | Creator Pool | Differentiator |
|-------------------|-------------|----------------|
| Aspire | Creator Marketplace | BOTH inbound (creators apply) + outbound (brand searches) — unique dual model |
| Later/Mavely | 85K+ affiliate creators | One-click affiliate links via 9 affiliate networks, automated commissions |
| Collabstr | 200K opt-in creators | Escrow, free tier, external brief sharing, UGC as distinct product |
| Afluencer | 24K–42K opt-in creators | Both sides free, inbound Collab model |
| Impact.com | Enterprise network | Marketplace-first, strongest conversion tracking |

**Cheerful opportunity**: Aspire and Later show that inbound creator acquisition + outbound discovery creates **a dual flywheel** — brands attract creators who apply, creating a warm pool that makes cold outreach unnecessary for established programs. An opt-in creator portal would compound Cheerful's Gmail-native outreach moat.

## Competitive Moat Summary

| **Gmail-native outreach** | Only Cheerful + Modash; every other competitor uses platform domain — measurably lower reply rates | Very High |
