## Pain Points to Address (from competitive research)

These are the verbatim pain points from Filipino freelancer communities that the landing page must speak to:

1. "I don't know if I should choose 8% or graduated" — regime confusion is the #1 question
2. "I spent ₱8,000 on a CPA for something that's just multiplication"
3. "I chose the wrong option and now I owe more tax and can't change it until next year"
4. "eBIRForms doesn't work on my Mac"
5. "I keep missing the quarterly deadlines (May 15, August 15, November 15)"
6. "My clients withhold tax but I don't know how to account for those 2307s"
7. "I'm employed AND freelancing — which form do I file?"

## Competitor Positioning (to differentiate from)

| Competitor | Their Hook | Their Weakness |
|------------|-----------|----------------|
| Taxumo | "File & Pay in Minutes" | Requires pre-selecting regime; ₱2,499/quarter minimum |
| JuanTax | "First BIR-Accredited Tax Platform" | Per-form pricing; B2B-oriented |
| TaxWhiz | "Tax Compliance Made Easy" | App-only; pricing opaque; advisory-dependent |
| eBIRForms | "Free from BIR" | Windows-only; no computation; manual math required |

## Value Proposition (to be refined in Wave 5)

**Primary**: "The only Philippine tax tool that tells you which tax regime saves you the most money."

**Secondary**: "Compute your exact tax under all three BIR-allowed methods. Know your savings before you commit."

**Tertiary**: "Stop paying CPAs ₱8,000/year for arithmetic. We do it in 30 seconds for free."

---

## Key Statistics for Social Proof

- 1.5 million registered Filipino freelancers
- 208% freelance economy growth in 2020 (Philippines led globally)
- CPA fee for annual ITR: ₱3,000–₱10,000
- Wrong regime choice = 20–40% tax overpayment
- Breakeven: 8% rate beats OSD (graduated) at EVERY income level below ₱3M (proven by computation in worked-examples.md EX-001 through EX-005)
- Irrevocable choice: regime must be elected in Q1 filing; cannot change for the rest of the year

## CTAs to Test

- Primary: "Compute My Tax Now — Free"
- Secondary: "See Which Regime Saves You More"
- Tertiary: "Try It Free — No Signup Required"

---

## Target Audiences

1. **First-time filer freelancer**: Just registered with BIR, confused about which regime to elect in Q1
2. **Returning freelancer**: Has been filing but suspects they've been overpaying
3. **Mixed-income earner**: Has a day job and freelances on the side; doesn't know how to handle both
4. **CPA / bookkeeper**: Handles multiple self-employed clients; wants batch comparison tool

## Competitor Weaknesses to Highlight (without naming names)

- "Unlike other tools, we don't ask you to choose a tax regime. We show you which one is cheaper."
- "No Windows required. No downloads. Works on any device."
- "Free to compute. Pay only if you want us to file for you."
- "One tool, all three regimes, side by side."
