## Freelance Tax Loop — Key Technical Findings

- Regime comparison breakeven: 8% vs itemized at ₱500K = 43.3% expense ratio (not 83% as originally estimated — CR-014 was wrong; PT must be included)
- OSD beats 8% only in narrow ₱400K–₱437.5K window (max ₱833 advantage)
- ₱250K exemption barred for 8% option when ANY compensation income exists (RMC 50-2018)
- EOPT Act (RA 11976, Jan 2024): shifted PT basis from cash to accrual, reduced MICRO/SMALL surcharge 25%→10%, interest 12%→6%
- At exactly ₱3M gross: SMALL tier, NOT VAT-required, 8% STILL eligible (3 different boundary expressions)
- Competitors: Taxumo (₱9,249/yr min, no regime comparison), JuanTax (₱120/form), TaxWhiz (opaque pricing), eBIRForms (free, Windows-only, no computation). None compute all 3 regimes simultaneously.
