# Vanessa - Small Items (52 total)

Items are listed with their tier progression (Bronze > Silver > Gold > Diamond) where values change across tiers.

Rating key:
- **S** = Must-pick in almost any build
- **A** = Strong pick, core in at least one archetype
- **B** = Good situational pick
- **C** = Filler, take if nothing better
- **D** = Generally avoid

---

## Ambergris
- **Size**: Small | **Types**: Aquatic, Economy, Heal, Value, Relic
- **Cooldown**: 7.0s
- **Effect**: Heal equal to x1/x2/x3/x4 Value. Gains +1/2/3/4 Value when buying Aquatic items.
- **Rating**: **A** -- Scales well in Aquatic-heavy builds. Self-funding heal source.
- **Synergies**: Any Aquatic build, Cove (Value scaling)

## Bayonet
