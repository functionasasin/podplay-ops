# Common Mistakes

## General Mistakes (All Heroes)

1. **Forcing a specific build** -- The #1 mistake. The Bazaar is about randomness. Play opportunistically based on what the game offers, not what you planned before the run.
2. **Overcommitting to short-term gains** -- Picking appealing items early without considering long-term build potential. That Bronze weapon looks nice now but won't scale.
3. **Ignoring PvE encounter difficulty** -- Selecting the hardest fights (rightmost option) without assessing build strength. Know your board's limits.
4. **Unfocused build construction** -- Attempting multiple strategies instead of optimizing around one cohesive theme. Splitting resources drops win rate by 35%+.
5. **Poor positioning** -- Failing to arrange items for adjacency synergies. Repositioning costs nothing -- do it every round.
6. **Not understanding keyword interactions** -- e.g., not knowing Poison bypasses Shield, or that Heal cleanses 5% Poison/Burn.
7. **Hoarding gold** -- Gold that sits in your bank isn't helping you win fights. Spend efficiently.
8. **Ignoring the stash** -- Start-of-Day and Each-Hour effects work from stash. Use this for income/economy items.

## Vanessa-Specific Mistakes

1. **Stocking too many Weapons without synergy** -- Many weapons with strong damage but no Healing/Shield support makes advanced days impossible.
2. **Forcing Weapons without an engine** -- If no good weapon appears early, immediately transition to defensive/aquatic. Don't stubbornly wait for weapons that may never come.
3. **Not pivoting from Weapons to Burn mid-game** -- Sometimes Weapons are best early, then pivot to Burn when you find Immolating Spark or Tracer Fire.
4. **Ignoring startup speed in late game** -- "Speed is everything in the late game." Ensure your first items fire as fast as possible (Holsters, Clamera, Haste skills).
5. **Not managing Ammo resources** -- Ammo depletes and needs recharging. New players forget this and wonder why their weapons stop firing.
6. **Trying to go past Day 10 with pure aggro** -- Vanessa's aggro builds are designed to close by Day 10. If you haven't won by then, you needed to transition.
7. **Ignoring Clamera** -- Always take Clamera. It's S-tier in every build. Fight-start Slow is universally powerful.
8. **Not taking Shuriken when offered** -- ALWAYS buy Shuriken. It's the #1 combo piece for late game Gunslinger.
9. **Upgrading items that break their synergy** -- Some items (e.g., Shorts) lose value when upgraded. Check before combining.
10. **Selling economy items too early** -- Star Chart, Wanted Poster, and Fishing Rod provide compounding value. Don't sell for a marginal board upgrade.
