## Vanessa Meta Position

Vanessa is **mid-tier (B)**. She's the most straightforward damage dealer and best beginner hero, but community consensus is:

- Viable and fun, but not the strongest pick for climbing ranked
- "Probably the weakest hero to pick at the moment, although the gap isn't big enough to not pick it if you enjoy this specific character" (TheBazaarZone)
- ~68% top-4 rate past Day 7 in high-rank lobbies (GameHelper data)
- Post-Patch 11.0: Weapon builds strengthened (Grappling Hook, Javelin buffs), Burn builds weakened (global Burn nerf)

### Vanessa's Best Builds (Ranked)

1. **Full Ammo Aggro / Gunslinger** -- Fastest, most reliable. Burst opponents before they scale.
2. **Anchor + Rowboat** -- Percentage-based damage, one-shot potential. Top-tier clock.
3. **Haste Poison (Aquatic)** -- Consistent scaling path. Vanessa's "bright side" in current meta.
4. **Slow Burn (Oni Mask)** -- Strong but took a hit from Burn nerfs. Still viable when pieces align.
5. **Tortuga Friend Engine** -- Niche but powerful when IllusoRay + Tortuga are available.

## Top Meta Builds Across All Heroes (Season 11)

### Dooley
- **Dooltron** -- Absolute powerhouse, flexibility + raw numbers
- **Harmadillo** -- Fast looping Shield triggers, Duct Tape revert buff
- **Ice 9000** -- Freeze disruption into scaling

### Pygmalien
- **Cargo Shorts** -- Absurd Multicast enabling combo builds
- **Landscraper** -- Fast Shield conversion archetype
- **Crook** -- Classic beatdown, now Bronze-tier for consistency

### Stelle
- **LavaRoller** -- Arguably strongest Burn build. Parts Picker creates thousands of Burn/sec
- **Self-Destroy** -- Charge > Destroy > Repair loops. Over-tuned
- **Pillbuggy** -- Flexible one-shot win condition

### Mak
- **Poppy Field** -- Aggressive Weapon payoff
- **Idol of Decay** -- Poison via infinite Charge loops
- **Runic Blade** -- Deadly one-shot build

### Jules
- **Freezer** -- Oppressively strong, positioning-dependent
- **Giant Lollipop** -- Fast with Slow control
- **Fruit Press** -- Exponential Food scaling
