# Vanessa Build Archetypes & Synergies

## Build Overview

Vanessa has four primary build archetypes, each with distinct win conditions and item requirements. The key to success is **committing to one archetype early** -- hybridizing before securing a core drops win rate by 35%+.

---

## Build 1: Gunslinger Aggro (Weapon + Ammo)

**Win condition**: Burst opponents down before they scale. Target 10 wins by Day 10.

### Core Items
| Item | Size | Role | Priority |
|------|------|------|----------|
| Sharkclaws + Katana | Med + Med | Classic opener, board-wide weapon buff | Early core |
| Orange Julian | Med | Highest ceiling damage scaler | #1 early priority |
| Bladed Hoverboard | Med | Passive damage on adjacent item use | Mid-game core |
| Pistol Sword | Med | Damage on every Ammo use | Late core |
| Shuriken | Small | #1 combo piece -- ALWAYS BUY when offered | Must-buy |
| Dive Weights | Small | Multicast equal to Ammo | Combo enabler |

### Support Items
- **Switchblade / Handaxe / Honing Steel**: Early weapon damage buffs
- **Double Barrel / Grenade**: Burst with Ammo mechanics
- **Powder Horn**: Ammo reload enabler
- **Holsters**: Fight start Haste for smalls
- **Lockbox / Figurehead**: Orange Julian alternatives

### Late-Game Capstones
- **Crow's Nest**: Board-wide Crit + Lifesteal for solo weapon
- **Ballista**: Scaling Multicast from Ammo use
- **Cannonade**: Self-charging from weapon use
- **Port**: Board-wide Ammo reload + daily items

### Key Principles
1. Orange Julian MUST activate before Multicast items (use Rowboat/Captain's Wheel for sequencing)
2. ALWAYS buy Shuriken when offered -- it's the #1 combo piece later
3. Star Chart at Bronze unlocks the 5 Gold + 1 XP Docks event
4. Prioritize Strength, Left-Handed, or Right-Handed skills early

### Board Variants
- All Smalls (Holsters + many small weapons)
- Holsters + Cutlass
- Lockbox combos
- Orange Julian packages
- Torpedo + Ballista

## Build 2: Burn / Slow Control

**Win condition**: Stack Burn while controlling enemy speed. Lighthouse and Darkwater Anglerfish are the engines.

### Core Items
| Item | Size | Role | Priority |
|------|------|------|----------|
| Lighthouse | Large | 20 Burn, charges from Slow | Build-defining |
| Darkwater Anglerfish | Med | Burn, charges from Slow | Build-defining |
| Dock Lines | Med | Multi-target Slow on 5s CD | S-tier enabler |
| Weather Glass | Med | Dual debuff + scaling Multicast | Core engine |
| Shot Glasses | Small | Haste + Slow in one item | Dual trigger |

### Support Items
- **Lighter / Pop Snappers / Volcanic Vents**: Early Burn sources
- **Clamera**: Fight-start Slow
- **Tripwire**: Passive enemy Slow (Fiery enchant target)
- **Zoarcid**: Haste + Burn charging
- **Incendiary Rounds**: Burn on adjacent use + Ammo buff
- **Oni Mask**: Weak early, powerful late

### Late-Game Capstones
- **Iceberg**: Passive Freeze on every use
- **Slumbering Primordial**: Converts Burn/Poison/Freeze triggers into massive damage

### Key Principles
1. Weather Glass needs minimum 4x Multicast to justify its slot
2. Fiery enchants on passive items (Korxena Crest, Tripwire) add significant Burn
3. Heavy enchants support Lighthouse builds through extra Slow triggers
4. Speed is critical -- fast startup prevents needing defensive enchants
5. Prioritize at least 6-8 Burn on single items to justify early investment

### Two Sub-Paths
- **Oni Mask Route**: Tracer Fire (Crit scaling) + Incendiary Rounds + Dive Weights
- **Lighthouse Route**: Dock Lines + Shot Glasses + Weather Glass + Nesting Doll

## Build 3: Haste / Poison (Aquatic Engine)

**Win condition**: Create self-reinforcing Haste + Poison loops that scale infinitely.

### Core Items
| Item | Size | Role | Priority |
|------|------|------|----------|
| Pufferfish | Med | THE Poison item, activates on ANY Haste | Core engine |
| Catfish | Small | Scales to 350+ Poison late game | Late-game engine |
| Jellyfish | Small | Poison + self-Haste from Aquatic use | Day 1 combo with Pufferfish |
| Shipwreck | Large | +1 Multicast on ALL Aquatic items | Game-changing capstone |
| Beach Ball | Med | Go-to Aquatic Haste enabler | Core support |

### Support Items
- **Astrolabe**: Self-charging Haste for non-Weapons (permanent Haste with Diamond Shipwreck)
- **Figurehead**: Aquatic cooldown reduction + weapon damage
- **Fishing Rod**: Daily free Aquatic + targeted Haste
- **Pearl**: Fastest looping Aquatic, massive trigger count
- **Diving Helmet**: Converts adjacent items to Aquatic
- **Yeti Crab**: Freeze + amplifies adjacent Poison items

### Defensive Layer
After assembling Poison + Haste core, add:
- Seaweed, Life Preserver, Coral Armor, Cove
- Clamera, IllusoRay, Harpoon (disruption)

### Key Principles
1. Pufferfish + Jellyfish is lethal from Day 1 -- take it whenever offered
2. Toxic enchant on Pufferfish DOUBLES its Poison
3. Early-mid game: Pufferfish is the engine. Day 13+: transition to Catfish (350+ Poison/cast)
4. Final Dose skill is near-core; Final Dose + Pufferfish is extremely potent early
5. With Diamond Shipwreck + Silver Astrolabe, most items get permanent Haste (6s CD or less)

### The Pufferfish + Trebuchet Trick
Enchanting Pufferfish with Burn and pairing with Trebuchet bypasses the internal cooldown, turning Pufferfish into a gatling gun of Poison/Burn.

## Build 4: Tortuga (Friend Engine)

**Win condition**: Spam Friend activations to charge Tortuga for massive repeated damage.

### Core Items
| Item | Size | Role | Priority |
|------|------|------|----------|
| Tortuga | Large | 200-800 Damage, Haste all, charges from Friends | Build-defining |
| Figurehead | Med | Aquatic CDR (most Friends are Aquatic) | Key enabler |
| IllusoRay | Small | Triple-charges Tortuga per activation | Core combo piece |
| Zoarcid | Small | Reliable Haste + Friend trigger | Core support |
| Pufferfish + Jellyfish | Med + Small | Tried and true Poison combo | Damage layer |

### Support Items
- **Orange Julian**: Fantastic damage scaling early
- **RED-F1R3FLY**: Between Zoarcid and Tortuga for synergistic value (from Shovel)
- **Sharkray / Old Saltclaw / Electric Eels**: Alternative Friend damage
- **Ambergris**: Value scaling in Aquatic-heavy boards
- **Insect Wings**: CDR to speed up Tortuga's Silver tier

### Skills
- **Inexorable**: Premium Crit Chance source
- **Captain's Charge**: Haste for startup
- **Stop That!**: Defensive Slow utility

### Key Principles
1. Tortuga charges 2s per Friend use -- spam as many Friends as possible
2. IllusoRay charges Tortuga THREE times per activation (insane synergy)
3. Figurehead accelerates the cycle since most Vanessa Friends are Aquatic
4. Electric Eels variant uses Sharkray + Old Saltclaw for different matchups
5. Build rewards exploration -- multiple viable variants

## Build 5: Slumbering Primordial (Trigger Spam)

**Win condition**: Convert spammy Burn/Poison/Freeze triggers into absurdly fast Primordial scaling.

### Core Items
| Item | Size | Role | Priority |
|------|------|------|----------|
| Slumbering Primordial | Large | Sole win condition, gains damage per debuff trigger | Must upgrade to Diamond |
| Incendiary Rounds | Small | Highest trigger count in the game | Essential |
| Dive Weights | Small | Multicast + Haste | Core combo |
| Holsters | Small | Fight-start Haste for smalls | Fast startup |
| Clamera | Small | Immediate trigger via Zoarcid | Startup optimization |

### Support Items
- **Nesting Doll**: 2s cooldown, charges Zoarcid quickly
- **Pop Snappers / Lighter**: Fast Burn sources
- **Zoarcid**: Haste + Burn charging
- **Custom Scope**: Optional Crit source
- **Iceberg**: Alternative Freeze disruption

### Skills
- **Heated Shells**: Key skill for fast trigger utilization
- **Oceanic Rush**: Startup improvement
- **Tracer Fire**: Crit scaling

### Key Principles
1. Slumbering Primordial is the SOLE win condition -- all other items serve it
2. Must upgrade to Diamond for late-game viability (charge speed increase is critical)
3. Holsters + Heated Shells configuration: Large item central with smalls surrounding it
4. Position Clamera adjacent to Zoarcid for immediate activation
5. Build doesn't need additional item/skill scaling beyond Primordial itself

## Key Synergy Chains

### Haste Chain
Beach Ball/Astrolabe/Water Wheel -> Pufferfish triggers -> Poison stacks -> Catfish amplifies -> Shipwreck multicasts everything

### Slow/Burn Chain
Dock Lines/Shot Glasses/Clamera -> Darkwater Anglerfish charges -> Lighthouse charges -> Burn stacks -> Weather Glass multicasts

### Ammo/Damage Chain
Powder Flask/Port -> Double Barrel/Pistol Sword -> Bladed Hoverboard triggers -> Dive Weights multicasts -> Ballista scales

### Friend/Charge Chain
IllusoRay/Zoarcid/Jellyfish (Friends) -> Tortuga charges -> Tortuga fires (200-800 damage) -> Haste all items -> loop repeats

### Debuff/Primordial Chain
Lighter/Incendiary Rounds (Burn) + Yeti Crab (Freeze) + Pufferfish (Poison) -> Slumbering Primordial charges -> gains damage per trigger -> fires for massive damage
