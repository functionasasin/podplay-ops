# PvE Monsters

Monster encounters appear at Hour 2 each day. Choose 1 of 3. Higher level = better rewards but harder fight.

## How to Look Up Monster Info

1. Search KB first: Grep for monster name in this file
2. If not found, check **thebazaar.wiki.gg**: `https://thebazaar.wiki.gg/wiki/{Monster_Name}`
3. If not found, check **bazaardb.gg**: search monsters category

### Flame Juggler
- **Level**: 6
- **HP**: 1200
- **Items**:
  - Lighter (Gold) — Burn 6, 3s CD
  - Magma Core (Gold) ×3 — Burn 12 at fight start each
  - Magma Core (Silver) ×2 — Burn 9 at fight start each
  - Cinders (Gold) — loot, no combat effect
- **Skills**: Burst of Flame (Silver) — Burn 15 when enemy drops below 50% HP
- **Total fight-start Burn**: 54 (36 + 18)
- **Sustained DPS**: ~100 from Burn (Lighter keeps stacks topped up)
- **Drops**: Cinders, Lighter, Magma Core
- **Rewards**: 5 Gold, 4 XP
- **WARNING**: Extremely dangerous without healing/shield. 54 fight-start Burn = ~309 damage in 3 seconds.

### Infernal Envoy
- **Level**: 6
- **HP**: 900
- **Items**:

  - Sunlight Spear — Burn + damage based on Regen, +4/8/12 Regen, 10-12s CD
- **Skills**: Vengeance (Silver) — leftmost and rightmost items cooldown reduced 5/10/15%
- **Drops**: Cinders, Fire Claw, Sunlight Spear

### Trashtown Mayor
- **Level**: 7
- **HP**: 1200
- **Items**:
  - Brass Knuckles — 8-32 damage, double damage modifier
  - Dog (Medium) — 10-80 damage, gains 3-12 damage per small item sold
  - Pepper Spray — 10-25 damage + Slow
  - Sharpening Stone — loot, grants 5-20 damage to leftmost weapon when sold
  - Temporary Shelter (Large) — 10-80 Shield, gains 5-20 Shield per small item sold
- **Skills**:
  - Augmented Defenses — Shields gain +1, +1/2 per small item sold
  - Augmented Weaponry — Weapons gain +1 damage, +1/2 per small item sold
- **Drops**: Augmented Weaponry (TOP-TIER weapon scaling skill), Brass Knuckles, Dog, Pepper Spray, Sharpening Stone, Temporary Shelter
- **Rewards**: 4 Gold, 2 XP
- **Note**: Items scale from selling smalls mid-fight. Has Shield + direct damage, not Burn.
