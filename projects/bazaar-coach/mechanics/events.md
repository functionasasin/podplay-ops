# Events & Encounters

Non-combat, non-merchant encounters that appear during hours. Choose one option per event.

---

## The Lost Crate

**Options:**
- **Open It**: Get a free Medium item
- **Return It**: Get a Bronze Skill

**Verdict**: Open It is almost always correct — a free Medium item on any day is huge value. Only take Return It if you desperately need a specific skill and have no other way to get one.

---

## Tiny Furry Monster

**Options:**
- **Pet It**: +25 Max Health
- **Scare It**: Get a Silver-tier Loot item
- **Feed It**: Choose 1 of 2 Skills (requires a Food item on board)
- **Play Hide and Seek**: Choose 1 of 2 Skills (requires a Friend item on board)
- **Play With It**: Choose 1 of 2 Skills (requires a Toy item on board)

**Verdict**: Skill options (Feed/Hide and Seek/Play) are best if available. Otherwise Scare It for the Silver loot item beats +25 HP in most cases.

---

## Candy Stash

**Options:**
- 3 Bronze Choco Bars (economy/sell items)

**Verdict**: Skip unless you need economy badly. Low value compared to other encounters.

---

## A Strange Mushroom

**Options:**
- **Trade It for Something**: Gain a Neutral Item
- **Sell It**: Gain 4 Gold
- **Add It to Your Bushel**: +20 Heal to Bushel (requires Bushel)
- **Brew a Potion**: Silver Potion (Mak only)
- **Share It With A Friend**: Silver Friend (Dooley only)

**Verdict**: Trade for neutral item or sell for 4g depending on build needs.

---

## Treasure Chest

**Options:**
- Get a free item (tier unknown — may scale with day)
- On Day 10+, you must fight the Mimic instead

**Verdict**: Free item with no downside before Day 10. Good default pick.

---

## Borrow

**Tier**: Silver event

**Options:**
- Lose 1 Income, gain Gold (scales by day):
  - Days 1-2: 8 Gold
  - Days 3-4: 7 Gold
  - Days 5-6: 6 Gold

**Verdict**: Only take if you need gold NOW for a key purchase. Losing income hurts long-term.

## Artisan Dunes

**Options** (applies to ALL items of that type, not just leftmost):
- **Apprentice Weaponsmith**: Weapons gain +5 Damage
- **Apprentice Flamewright**: Burn items gain +1 Burn
- **Apprentice Banehand**: Poison items gain +1 Poison
- **Find Better Craftsman**: Doubles the chosen effect

**Verdict**: Always take Double. Then pick based on board — compare total value across all items of each type. Burn scales quadratically so +2 Burn can outvalue +10 Damage in Burn-heavy builds.

---

## Gumball Machine

**Options:**
- Buy gumballs for 1 Gold each (max 10)
- Colors: Red, Blue, Yellow, Green (each has different sell effect)
- Blue Gumball is often most valuable (crit-related)

**Verdict**: Cheap investment if you have spare gold. Blue gumballs for crit are standout.

## Fate's Crossroads (Prestige = 0)

Triggers when Prestige hits 0. One final opportunity before elimination:
- **Random Diamond-tier item** — potentially build-defining
- **Random enchantment** — Heavy and Icy are especially powerful here
- **20 Gold + 5 XP** — safe, flexible option
