# Vanessa

## Overview

Vanessa is The Bazaar's most aggressive hero, with the tools to strike first and hard. She has the widest range of weaponry, Ammo mechanics, and Aquatic friends of any hero. She excels at early aggression but offers surprising build diversity.

**Win condition target**: 10 wins as close to Day 10 as possible, before opponents out-scale her early aggression.

## Hero Identity

- **Archetype**: Aggressive / Burst / DoT
- **Signature traits**: Weapons, Ammo, Aquatic friends, Poison, Burn
- **Item pool size**: ~130 items (52 Small, 57 Medium, 21 Large)
- **Flexibility**: High -- three distinct build paths with multiple variants each

## Build Archetypes

### 1. Gunslinger / Burst Aggro (Weapon + Ammo)
**Win condition**: Overwhelm opponents with fast-casting, high-damage weapons before they can scale.

**Core items**: Double Barrel, Sharkclaws, Katana, Pistol Sword, Bladed Hoverboard, Orange Julian
**Key support**: Powder Horn, Crow's Nest, Figurehead, Holsters, Silencer
**Late-game capstones**: Ballista, Cannonade, Flagship

**Key principle**: Stack Crit to bypass ammo limitations through sheer damage. Orange Julian is the highest early-game priority with absurdly high ceiling. ALWAYS buy Shuriken when offered -- it's the #1 combo piece for late game.

### 2. Burn / Slow (DoT Control)
**Win condition**: Stack Burn and Poison effects for scaling damage over extended fights while controlling enemy speed.

**Core items**: Weather Glass, Trebuchet, Lighthouse, Darkwater Anglerfish, Volcanic Vents
**Key support**: Lighter, Pop Snappers, Dock Lines, Shot Glasses, Zoarcid, Incendiary Rounds
**Late-game capstones**: Slumbering Primordial, Iceberg

**Key principle**: Weather Glass needs minimum 4x Multicast to be effective. Fiery enchants on passive items (Korxena Crest, Tripwire) boost offense significantly.

### 3. Aquatic / Poison (Haste Loop)
**Win condition**: Create self-reinforcing Haste + Poison loops that scale infinitely.

**Core items**: Pufferfish, Catfish, Jellyfish, Shipwreck, Beach Ball
**Key support**: Astrolabe, Figurehead, Fishing Rod, Diving Helmet, Rowboat
**Late-game capstones**: Tortuga, Seadog's Saloon, Water Wheel

**Key principle**: Pufferfish activates whenever ANY item on board is hasted -- it's the core Poison engine. Catfish can reach 350+ Poison per cast late game. Jellyfish + Pufferfish combo can get running as early as Day 1.

### 4. Aquatic Vehicles (Single-Weapon Focus)
**Win condition**: Concentrate on one vehicle weapon, unlocking passive bonuses through supporting items.

**Core items**: Flagship, Submarine, Torpedo, Captain's Wheel
**Key support**: Silencer, tools, ammo items, properties, friends (for Flagship Multicast)

## Power Spikes

| Day | What happens |
|-----|-------------|
| 1-3 | Establish direction: weapon opener OR aquatic engine |
| 3-4 | **Critical**: Securing a Silver item here increases late-game viability by 200%+ |
| 5-7 | Core engine should be online; commit to archetype |
| 7-10 | Win race -- close out games before opponents outscale |
| 10+ | If still alive, need Diamond-tier capstone (Shipwreck, Tortuga, Slumbering Primordial) |

## Item Priority (S-Tier Must-Picks)

These items are almost always correct to take regardless of build:
- **Clamera** -- Slow at fight start, universally strong
- **Shipwreck** -- Aquatic items gain +1 Multicast, enables everything
- **Wanted Poster** -- XP + Crit, always valuable
- **Dive Weights** -- Haste + Multicast, enables combos
- **Star Chart** -- Crit + Cooldown reduction, universally good
- **Rowboat** -- Charges adjacent items, flexible
- **Anchor** -- % Max Health damage, scales into late game
- **Pistol Sword** -- Damage on every Ammo use, weapon builds love it
- **The Boulder** -- Deals damage equal to opponent's Max Health
- **Dock Lines** -- Multi-target Slow
- **Weather Glass** -- Burn + Poison + scaling Multicast
- **Life Preserver** -- Shield + death prevention
- **Lighthouse** -- High Burn, charges when Slowing
- **Darkwater Anglerfish** -- Burn that charges when Slowing
- **Iceberg** -- Freezes enemy items when used
- **Lighter** -- Cheap, fast Burn source
- **Shot Glasses** -- Haste + Slow in one item

## What to Avoid

- **Do NOT force weapon builds without an engine** -- if no good weapon appears early, pivot to defensive/aquatic
- **Do NOT hybridize archetypes** before securing a core -- win rate drops 35%+ when splitting focus
- **Watch out for upgrade traps** -- some items (e.g., Shorts) can break their synergy loop when upgraded
- **Avoid generic filler** -- items that don't advance your archetype are dead board space

## Enchantment Priorities

| Enchantment | Best on | Effect |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| Fiery | Passive items (Korxena Crest, Tripwire) | Adds Burn |
| Toxic | Pufferfish, damage dealers | Doubles Poison / adds Poison |
| Heavy | Lighthouse build items | Adds Slow triggers |
| Turbo | Engine pieces | Doubles Haste duration |
| Shielded | Weapons | Shield equal to damage |
| Deadly | Weapons, Crit items | Adds Crit Chance |
